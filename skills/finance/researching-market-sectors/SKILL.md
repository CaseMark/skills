# Researching Market Sectors

Creates comprehensive sector research packages.

## Workflow

1. **Define** sector boundaries — what's in, what's adjacent
2. **Size** the market — TAM, SAM, SOM with methodology
3. **Map** competitive landscape — key players, market share, positioning
4. **Identify** trends — growth drivers, headwinds, disruption vectors
5. **Analyze** value chain — where value is created and captured
6. **Assess** investability — sector attractiveness framework

## Key Rules

- Market sizing: always show methodology and sources — top-down AND bottom-up
- TAM is not the opportunity — SAM and SOM matter more for investment
- Market share data is often estimated — note confidence level
- Trends: distinguish cyclical from structural changes
- Disruption vectors: technology, regulation, business model innovation
- Adjacent markets can be both threats and expansion opportunities
