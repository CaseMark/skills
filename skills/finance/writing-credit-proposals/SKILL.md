# Writing Credit Proposals

Builds credit committee-ready lending proposals.

## Workflow

1. **Summarize** the request — borrower, amount, purpose, structure
2. **Analyze** borrower — financial condition, management, industry
3. **Assess** repayment capacity — cash flow, collateral, guarantees
4. **Structure** the deal — terms, covenants, pricing
5. **Evaluate** risk — rating, mitigants, stress scenarios
6. **Recommend** — approve/decline with conditions

## Key Rules

- Three sources of repayment: primary (cash flow), secondary (collateral), tertiary (guarantees)
- Stress test cash flow: what happens if revenue drops 20%?
- Debt service coverage ratio must be calculated on projected AND stressed basis
- Pricing must reflect risk — document the risk-return rationale
- Existing exposure: include total relationship and concentration analysis
- Exception tracking: any policy exceptions must be explicitly noted
