# Preparing Board Materials

Creates governance-appropriate board presentation content.

## Workflow

1. **Executive** summary — key developments since last meeting
2. **Financial** dashboard — performance vs. budget, prior year, forecast
3. **Strategic** updates — progress on initiatives, competitive developments
4. **KPIs** — metric tracking against targets
5. **Risk** update — new/changed risks, mitigation status
6. **Decision** items — what needs board approval, with recommendations

## Output Template

```markdown
## Board Meeting Materials: [Date]

### Agenda
1. [Item — Time allocation]

### Executive Summary
[1-page overview: what happened, what's coming, what needs attention]

### Financial Dashboard
| Metric | Actual | Budget | Variance | Prior Year | YoY |
|--------|--------|--------|----------|-----------|-----|

### KPI Scorecard
| KPI | Target | Actual | Status | Trend |
|-----|--------|--------|--------|-------|

### Strategic Initiative Updates
| Initiative | Owner | Status | Key Milestones | Issues |
|-----------|-------|--------|---------------|--------|

### Risk Register Update
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Trend | Mitigation Status |
|------|------------|--------|-------|-------------------|

### Items Requiring Board Action
| # | Item | Recommendation | Materials Reference |
|---|------|---------------|-------------------|

### Appendix
[Detailed financials, supporting data]
```

## Key Rules

- Board members have limited time — lead with the "so what"
- Financial variance explanations for any item >10% off budget
- Traffic light status (green/yellow/red) for quick scanning
- Decision items: present recommendation, not just options
- Risk updates: focus on what changed since last meeting
- Compensation and related-party items in separate executive session section
