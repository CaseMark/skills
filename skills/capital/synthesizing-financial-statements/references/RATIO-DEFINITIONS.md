# Financial Ratio Definitions

## Liquidity Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | Ability to pay short-term obligations. >1.5 generally healthy; varies by industry |
| Quick Ratio | (Cash + Short-term Investments + Receivables) / Current Liabilities | Liquidity excluding inventory. >1.0 preferred |
| Cash Ratio | (Cash + Short-term Investments) / Current Liabilities | Most conservative liquidity measure |
| Operating Cash Flow Ratio | Operating Cash Flow / Current Liabilities | Cash-based liquidity (more reliable than accrual-based) |

## Profitability Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Pricing power and production efficiency |
| Operating Margin | Operating Income / Revenue | Core business profitability before interest and taxes |
| Net Margin | Net Income / Revenue | Bottom-line profitability |
| EBITDA Margin | EBITDA / Revenue | Cash-proxy profitability, useful for comparisons across capital structures |
| ROE | Net Income / Average Shareholders' Equity | Return generated on equity investment |
| ROA | Net Income / Average Total Assets | How efficiently assets generate profit |
| ROIC | NOPAT / Average Invested Capital | Return on all capital (debt + equity); best profitability metric |

**NOPAT** = Operating Income × (1 - Tax Rate)
**Invested Capital** = Total Equity + Total Debt - Cash

## Leverage Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Debt-to-Equity | Total Debt / Total Equity | Capital structure; higher = more leveraged |
| Debt-to-Assets | Total Debt / Total Assets | Portion of assets financed by debt |
| Debt-to-EBITDA | Total Debt / EBITDA | Debt relative to earnings; <3x generally healthy |
| Net Debt / EBITDA | (Total Debt - Cash) / EBITDA | Leverage net of cash; most common credit metric |
| Interest Coverage | EBIT / Interest Expense | Ability to service debt; >3x preferred |
| Fixed Charge Coverage | (EBIT + Lease Payments) / (Interest + Lease Payments) | Broader coverage including lease obligations |

## Efficiency Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| Asset Turnover | Revenue / Average Total Assets | Revenue generated per dollar of assets |
| Inventory Turnover | COGS / Average Inventory | How quickly inventory is sold |
| Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) | (Average Inventory / COGS) × 365 | Days to sell inventory |
| Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) | (Average Receivables / Revenue) × 365 | Days to collect receivables |
| Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) | (Average Payables / COGS) × 365 | Days to pay suppliers |
| Cash Conversion Cycle | DSO + DIO - DPO | Days between paying for inputs and collecting from customers |

## Valuation Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Interpretation |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| P/E | Price per Share / EPS | Price relative to earnings; compare to peers and growth |
| EV/EBITDA | Enterprise Value / EBITDA | Capital-structure-neutral valuation; most common comp metric |
| EV/Revenue | Enterprise Value / Revenue | Used for high-growth or unprofitable companies |
| P/B | Price per Share / Book Value per Share | Price relative to accounting equity |
| PEG | P/E / Earnings Growth Rate | P/E adjusted for growth; <1 may indicate undervaluation |
| FCF Yield | Free Cash Flow per Share / Price per Share | Cash return to shareholders at current price |
| Dividend Yield | Annual Dividend per Share / Price per Share | Income return |

**Enterprise Value** = Market Cap + Total Debt - Cash + Minority Interest + Preferred Stock
