# Assessing Regulatory Exposure

Maps business activities to regulatory requirements.

## Workflow

1. **Profile** business activities — products, services, customer types, geographies
2. **Map** to regulators — which agencies have jurisdiction
3. **Identify** applicable regulations — specific rules and requirements
4. **Assess** compliance obligations — licensing, reporting, conduct rules
5. **Gap** analysis — what's in place vs. what's required
6. **Prioritize** remediation by risk and regulatory urgency

## Key Rules

- Regulatory overlap is common — multiple agencies may regulate the same activity
- State-by-state licensing requirements for money transmission, lending, insurance
- New products may trigger new regulatory requirements — assess before launch
- Fintech activities often have unclear regulatory boundaries — note ambiguity
- Enforcement trends matter — what are regulators currently focused on?
- Cost of non-compliance includes fines, cease-and-desist, and reputational damage
