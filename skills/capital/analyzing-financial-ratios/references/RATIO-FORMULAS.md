# Financial Ratio Formulas and Industry Benchmarks

## Formulas with Calculation Notes

### Profitability

| Ratio | Formula | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100 | Excludes D&A if classified in COGS |
| EBITDA | Operating Income + D&A | Or: Net Income + Tax + Interest + D&A |
| EBITDA Margin | EBITDA / Revenue × 100 | — |
| ROIC | NOPAT / Avg Invested Capital | NOPAT = EBIT × (1 - Tax Rate); Invested Capital = Equity + Debt - Cash |
| DuPont ROE | Net Margin × Asset Turnover × Equity Multiplier | Decomposes ROE into profitability, efficiency, leverage |

### Leverage

| Ratio | Formula | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Net Debt | Total Debt - Cash - Short-term Investments | Include capital leases in debt |
| Net Debt / EBITDA | Net Debt / LTM EBITDA | Most common credit metric |
| Interest Coverage | EBIT / Interest Expense | Or EBITDA / Interest for cash-based |
| Debt / Total Cap | Total Debt / (Total Debt + Equity) | Capitalization ratio |

### Cash Flow

| Ratio | Formula | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Free Cash Flow | Operating CF - CapEx | Some definitions subtract dividends |
| FCF Conversion | FCF / Net Income | >100% is strong; <70% is weak |
| FCF Yield | FCF / Enterprise Value × 100 | Or: FCF per share / share price |
| CapEx Intensity | CapEx / Revenue × 100 | Higher = more capital-intensive business |

**Note**: Ranges are approximate mid-cycle benchmarks for US large-cap companies. Actual ranges vary by market conditions, company size, geography, and business model.

## Sector-Specific Metrics

| Sector | Key Metrics |
|--------|-----------|
| **SaaS** | ARR, NRR (>120% excellent), CAC Payback, Rule of 40 (growth + margin) |
| **Banks** | NIM, Efficiency Ratio (<60% good), CET1 (>10%), NPL Ratio, ROTCE |
| **REITs** | FFO/AFFO, NAV discount/premium, Occupancy Rate, Cap Rate, Debt/Assets |
| **Insurance** | Combined Ratio (<100% = underwriting profit), Investment Income Yield |
| **Airlines** | RASM, CASM, Load Factor, Available Seat Miles |
| **Hospitals** | Revenue per Adjusted Patient Day, Payor Mix, Case Mix Index |
