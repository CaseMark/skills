# Screening Investment Opportunities

Applies structured filters to identify investment candidates.

## Workflow

1. **Define** universe — asset class, geography, sector, size constraints
2. **Apply** quantitative filters — financial metrics, valuation, momentum
3. **Apply** qualitative filters — management, competitive position, ESG
4. **Score** survivors — weighted scoring against criteria
5. **Rank** and shortlist — top candidates for deep analysis
6. **Document** passes — why each candidate was included/excluded

## Key Rules

- Screen criteria should map to investment thesis — not arbitrary cutoffs
- Multiple screens in sequence reduce universe systematically (funnel approach)
- Document why each threshold was chosen
- Track companies that narrowly missed — they may improve
- Avoid over-screening: too many criteria can eliminate all candidates
- Quantitative screens are starting points, not conclusions
