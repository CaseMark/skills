# Compliance Summary

Produces a governance-ready compliance summary assessing regulatory posture, identifying material gaps, and mapping a prioritized remediation roadmap.

## Prerequisites

1. **Industry and jurisdiction** — confirm sector (finance, healthcare, environmental, data privacy, other) and geographic footprint (federal, state, international)
2. **Source documents** — compliance policies, internal audit reports, regulatory correspondence, incident reports, prior compliance summaries, consent orders or settlement agreements
3. **Scope definition** — confirm whether summary covers full enterprise, specific business unit, or defined regulatory domain

## Output Structure

### 1. Executive Summary

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Overall Posture | Compliant / Substantially Compliant (minor gaps) / Non-Compliant (material deficiencies) / Under Active Regulatory Scrutiny |
| Top 3 Risks | Ranked by severity + regulatory exposure |
| Immediate Action Items | Items requiring executive or board attention now |
| Review Period | Date range covered by this assessment |

---

### 2. Regulatory Requirements Matrix

For each applicable requirement:

| Requirement | Citation | Obligation | Responsible Party | Deadline/Frequency | Penalty Exposure |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Reg name] | [Cite] | [What's required] | [Dept/role] | [Date/cadence] | [Fine/enforcement risk] |

Organize by regulatory domain (e.g., SEC/FINRA, HIPAA/FDA, EPA, CCPA/GDPR) or by business unit.

---

### 3. Compliance Status Assessment

For each requirement in the matrix:

- **Status**: Compliant | Gap Identified | Deficiency | Unknown/Insufficient Evidence
- **Supporting Evidence**: Policies, training records, audit results, certifications, regulatory filings
- **Gap Description**: Specific deficiency with factual basis
- **Remediation**: Action steps, owner, target completion date, resource estimate

---

## Guidelines

- **Cite precisely**: Include CFR sections, statute numbers, and agency guidance document identifiers; flag uncertain citations with `[VERIFY]`
- **Distinguish evidence quality**: Separate documented compliance from self-reported or assumed compliance
- **Flag gray areas**: Note regulatory interpretive uncertainty; recommend proactive regulator engagement or outside counsel review where applicable
- **Emerging trends**: Flag anticipated regulatory changes requiring future program modification
- **Dual-audience language**: Executive summary must be accessible to non-lawyers; detailed sections may use technical regulatory terminology
- **No legal advice**: Frame as compliance assessment; note where legal counsel review is required before reliance
