# Receipt of FDD Form

Drafts an execution-ready Receipt of Franchise Disclosure Document that establishes an unambiguous evidentiary record of FTC Franchise Rule compliance for use in regulatory audits, state examinations, or litigation.

## Prerequisites

1. **Franchisor identity** — exact legal entity name and principal business address as it appears on the FDD cover page
2. **Franchisee identity** — full legal name (individual or entity), address, contact information; if entity, include formation state and authorized signer
3. **FDD details** — issuance date, version/amendment designations, state registration numbers if applicable
4. **Exhibit inventory** — list of all exhibits, attachments, addenda, and ancillary agreements delivered with the FDD
5. **Jurisdiction check** — whether franchisor operates in registration states (CA, NY, IL, MD, MN, VA, WA, WI, etc.) requiring additional acknowledgment language

## Output Structure

### Document Sections

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **Header** | "Receipt of Franchise Disclosure Document" title; franchisor legal name; principal address; document date (spell out month) |
| **Franchisee ID** | Full legal name; entity type/state of formation if applicable; address; phone; email; authorized signer name and title if entity |
| **Acknowledgment** | First-person attestation covering all items in the acknowledgment checklist below |
| **FDD Specification** | FDD issuance date; version/revision; state registration numbers; complete exhibit list |
| **Signatures** | Dual signature blocks (franchisee + franchisor representative) with E-SIGN/UETA language |

### Acknowledgment Checklist

The franchisee's attestation must confirm each of the following:

- [ ] Received complete FDD including all exhibits, attachments, financial performance representations (if any), and state-specific addenda on **[spelled-out date]**
- [ ] Understands the 14-calendar-day waiting period before signing any agreement or making any payment (except permitted escrow deposits under 16 C.F.R. § 436)
- [ ] Has been afforded opportunity to consult legal counsel, accountants, and other advisors
- [ ] No oral or written representations have been made that contradict or supplement the FDD
- [ ] **If Item 19 FPR exists:** Received and reviewed financial performance representations and understands their limitations
- [ ] **If no Item 19 FPR:** Understands franchisor has not provided earnings claims and will not rely on any unauthorized claims

### Signature Block Template

```
FRANCHISEE:

Signature: ________________________
Printed Name: _____________________
Title (if entity): ________________
Date: _____________________________

FRANCHISOR: [Legal Entity Name]

Signature: ________________________
Printed Name: _____________________
Title: _____________________________
Date: _____________________________
```

Include counterpart execution clause and E-SIGN Act / UETA electronic signature authorization if applicable.

## Guidelines

- **Date format**: Always spell out the month (e.g., "January 15, 2025") — never use numeric-only formats
- **Entity signers**: Distinguish "individually" vs. "as [Title] of [Entity Name]" in signature blocks
- **Registration states**: Add state-specific addenda acknowledgment language for CA, NY, IL, MD, MN, VA, WA, WI, and other registration jurisdictions — check uploaded documents for applicable states
- **Completeness**: Cross-reference the FDD's table of contents to verify the exhibit list is exhaustive
- **Minimal placeholders**: Insert transaction-specific data from uploaded documents wherever available; use bracketed placeholders only for genuinely unknown fields
- **Retention**: Format for both physical filing and electronic archival (standard fonts, 1-inch margins, page numbering if multi-page)
- **Regulatory scope**: 16 C.F.R. § 436 governs federal requirements; always check for additional state-level timing or content mandates
