## Prerequisites

1. Complete, executed agreement and all exhibits/schedules
2. Amendments, side letters, or addenda
3. Any referenced policies or incorporated documents
4. Definitions section (or a list of key defined terms)
5. Business context if provided (product, market, commercialization plan)

## Guidelines

- Prioritize contract language over assumptions; mark missing terms as `Not specified`.
- Quote defined terms exactly; do not paraphrase defined scope or exclusivity.
- Flag unusual clauses: MFN, grant-back, no-challenge, broad audit rights, termination at will.
- Call out any inconsistency between exhibits and the main body.
- Assume U.S. context unless the agreement specifies otherwise.
- Do not provide legal advice; summarize and flag issues only.
