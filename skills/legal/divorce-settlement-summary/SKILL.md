# Divorce Settlement Summary

Create a neutral, litigation-ready summary of a divorce settlement agreement with all material terms and action items.

## Prerequisites

1. **Executed or draft settlement agreement** (including attachments, exhibits, schedules)
2. **Case identifiers** (party names, case number, court/jurisdiction, settlement date)
3. **Parenting plan / custody exhibit** (if separate)
4. **Financial schedules** (asset/debt lists, QDRO details, support worksheets, if referenced)

## Guidelines

- Maintain strict neutrality; no fairness analysis or advocacy.
- Capture every material term; avoid boilerplate repetition.
- Use exact figures, dates, and percentages when stated.
- Flag missing data or cross-referenced schedules as “not provided.”
- Note jurisdiction-specific terms without redefining them; custody and support rules are state-specific.
- If any legal citation is referenced and uncertain, mark as `[VERIFY]`.
