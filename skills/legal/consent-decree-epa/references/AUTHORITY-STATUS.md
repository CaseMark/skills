## Notes on use

- The penalty figures in this Authority Status table are frozen at the verification date. **Always pull current values from 40 CFR § 19.4 (eCFR) at draft time.** EPA publishes the annual inflation adjustment in early calendar year; numbers in EPA enforcement letters and DOJ press releases lag by weeks at the start of each year.
- For PFAS-specific penalty matters, EPA's CERCLA PFAS Enforcement Discretion Policy may affect federal pursuit of certain parties, but private cost-recovery (CERCLA § 113(f)) is not constrained by that policy.
