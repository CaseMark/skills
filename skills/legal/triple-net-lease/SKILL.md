# Commercial Lease Agreement (Triple Net)

Produces a transaction-ready NNN commercial lease with precise expense allocation, remedies, and execution mechanics.

## Prerequisites

1. **Parties** — legal names, entity types, formation states, addresses, authorized signatories.
2. **Premises** — street address, suite/unit, rentable area standard (e.g., BOMA), legal description, parcel/APN, parking and common-area rights.
3. **Term Sheet/LOI** — business terms, rent schedule, escalations, TI, concessions, options.
4. **Property Context** — single-tenant vs multi-tenant, CAM structure, exclusive use limits, CC&Rs.
5. **Use & Compliance** — permitted use, zoning confirmation, licensing needs, ADA allocation.
6. **Insurance Targets** — required coverages, limits, carrier ratings, additional insureds.
7. **Finance/Title** — lender requirements, SNDA, subordination expectations.
8. **Exhibits** — site plan, rules/CAM, work letter, estoppel form, SNDA form.

## Guidelines

- Use **specific permitted use** language; avoid vague “retail/office use” unless intentional.
- Confirm **statutory notice and cure periods** and **mitigation duties** in the property’s state.
- If including **jury trial waiver**, ensure conspicuous formatting and compliance with local enforceability rules.
- Avoid indemnity language that violates **anti-indemnity statutes** for landlord negligence.
- Set **interest on late payments** at the maximum lawful rate for the jurisdiction.
- For multi-tenant properties, **exclude capital improvements** from CAM unless amortized.
- Clarify **tax appeal rights** and allocation of savings/costs.
- If lease term exceeds local recordation thresholds, include **recording memorandum** and notary requirements.
- Keep definitions and cross-references consistent; run a final defined-term audit.
