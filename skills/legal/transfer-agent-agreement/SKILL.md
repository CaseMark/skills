# Transfer Agent Agreement

Draft a transfer agent agreement that defines appointment, services, compliance duties, fees, and transition mechanics.

## Prerequisites

1. Issuer legal name, entity type, state of incorporation, principal address, and authorized signatory.
2. Transfer agent legal name, principal address, SEC registration status and number, and authorized signatory.
3. Securities covered: class/series, par value, CUSIP (if any), legends and transfer restrictions.
4. Issuer profile: public/private, SEC reporting status, exchange/DTC/FAST/DRS participation, approximate holder count.
5. Service scope: book-entry vs certificated, paying agent duties, corporate action support, escheatment handling.
6. Transfer processing rules: required documentation, medallion guarantees, legal opinions, stop transfer list authority.
7. Fees: retainer, per-transfer fees, per-certificate fees, onboarding charges, volume tiers, expense caps.
8. Term, renewal, termination notice periods, and transition timeline.
9. Data security/privacy requirements and breach notification timing.
10. Dispute resolution preference, governing law, and venue.
11. Exhibits to attach: fee schedule, service levels, forms, specimen certificates (if used).

Transfer Processing Checklist:
- Confirm authorized signers list and instruction channel.
- Require medallion signature guarantee for certificated transfers.
- Define acceptable legal opinions for legend removal.
- Maintain and honor stop transfer list.
- Define rejection notice timing and cure path.
- Define turnaround time for routine transfers.

Core Clause Templates:
```text
Appointment. Issuer appoints Transfer Agent as [exclusive/non-exclusive] transfer agent and registrar for the Securities. Transfer Agent accepts and will perform the Services in accordance with applicable law and this Agreement.
```

```text
Transfer Restrictions. Transfer Agent will refuse any transfer that violates applicable securities laws, contractual restrictions, or Issuer instructions. Legend removal requires (a) Issuer written authorization and (b) legal opinion from counsel reasonably acceptable to Issuer, unless Issuer waives in writing.
```

```text
Transition Assistance. Upon termination, Transfer Agent will deliver complete Records in a mutually agreed electronic format and provide transition services for [30/60] days. Issuer will pay all undisputed fees and reasonable transition expenses.
```

Fee Schedule Template:
```text
Retainer: $[amount] per [month/year]
Per-transfer: $[amount] per transfer
Per-certificate issue: $[amount]
Replacement certificate: $[amount]
Onboarding/Conversion: $[amount] (one-time)
Expenses: Reimbursable at cost; pre-approval required above $[threshold]
Billing: [monthly/quarterly], net [30] days
```

## Guidelines

- Confirm SEC transfer agent registration and require covenant to maintain registration; include loss of registration as termination trigger.
- Specify whether the appointment is exclusive and whether future issuances are covered by default.
- Match transfer restriction mechanics to the issuer's actual legends and lockups; avoid generic legend language.
- Align recordkeeping duties with the issuer's state corporate law requirements for stock ledger maintenance.
- Keep indemnities mutual and exclude coverage for the indemnified party's gross negligence or willful misconduct.
- Cap liability only if carve-outs preserve confidentiality, data breaches, and willful misconduct.
- If public/DTC participant, address DTC/FAST/DRS interfaces and corporate action timelines.
- If private, emphasize consent rights, ROFR/ROFO procedures, and issuer approval steps.
- Use [VERIFY] on any uncertain rule citations; confirm current SEC transfer agent rules before finalizing.
- Do not draft legal opinions; only specify when they are required and acceptable form.
