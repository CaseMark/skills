# Employment Contract Summary

Create a concise, citation-backed summary of an employment agreement with risks and gaps flagged.

## Prerequisites

1. **Complete agreement set** — main contract plus exhibits, schedules, equity plans, and referenced policies.
2. **Governing law and venue** — from the document; if missing, mark as absent.
3. **Role context** — job title, level (executive/non‑executive), and start date if known.

## Guidelines

- Cite every material term with section/page references.
- Quantify everything (dollars, dates, percentages, vesting schedules).
- Distinguish “silent” vs. “expressly excluded.”
- Flag non‑competes and non‑solicits as state‑dependent; do not assume enforceability.
- Do not provide legal advice or outcome predictions; only flag issues and conflicts.
