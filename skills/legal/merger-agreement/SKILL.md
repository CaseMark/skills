# Merger Agreement

Produce a signing-to-closing merger agreement that reflects the agreed structure, economics, and risk allocation.

## Prerequisites

1. **Parties and structure** — legal names, formation jurisdictions, surviving entity, merger type.
2. **Consideration** — cash/stock mix, exchange ratio, earnout/contingent terms, escrow/holdback.
3. **Capitalization** — cap tables, equity awards, convertibles, appraisal rights applicability.
4. **Diligence inputs** — financials, material contracts, IP, litigation, regulatory items.
5. **Regulatory/tax plan** — antitrust, securities, foreign investment review, tax treatment intent.
6. **Timeline and approvals** — board/stockholder approvals, outside date, conditions, financing (if any).
7. **Ancillary docs** — voting agreements, support letters, employment/retention, escrow, registration rights.

## Output Structure / Process

1. **Confirm transaction map** — parties, merger steps, filings, and closing sequence.
2. **Draft mechanics and economics** — effective time, consideration flow, equity conversion.
3. **Allocate risk** — reps, covenants, conditions, termination, fees, indemnification.
4. **Add governance and post-close** — board/officers, employee matters, transitional covenants.
5. **Attach schedules/exhibits** — disclosure schedules, charter/bylaws, forms of ancillary agreements.
6. **Quality pass** — defined terms, cross-references, closing deliverables, conflicts with schedules.

| Section | Required Inputs | Drafting Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Preamble/Recitals | Parties, purpose, structure | Identify merger form and intended effective time. |
| Definitions | Deal-specific terms | Define MAE, Knowledge, Permitted Liens, Material Contracts. |
| Merger Mechanics | Surviving entity, filings | Specify filings and effect on charter/bylaws. |
| Consideration | Price, exchange ratio | Include cash/stock mix and payment mechanics. |
| Equity Treatment | Options/RSUs/convertibles | Address vesting, cancellation, assumption. |
| Reps & Warranties | Diligence scope | Use schedules to qualify; set materiality/knowledge. |
| Covenants | Interim operations | Ordinary course, no-shop/go-shop, access. |
| Conditions | Regulatory, approvals | Mutual and party-specific conditions. |
| Termination | Outside date, breaches | Include fees and cure periods. |
| Indemnification | Survival, caps | Align with escrow/holdback; exclusive remedy. |
| Tax | Transaction intent | State intended treatment only if confirmed. |
| Miscellaneous | Governing law, notices | Include specific performance, assignment limits. |

**Equity Treatment Matrix**

| Security | Treatment at Effective Time | Payment/Delivery | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Common Stock | Convert into Merger Consideration | Payable at closing | Address fractional shares. |
| Preferred Stock | Convert per liquidation preference or negotiated ratio | Payable at closing | Confirm approval thresholds. |
| Options | Cancel for spread or assume/convert | At closing or vesting | Specify exercise/withholding. |
| RSUs/PSUs | Vest/cancel/assume | At closing or per award | Address performance targets. |
| Warrants/Convertibles | Convert or terminate | Per instrument | Require consents if needed. |

**Closing Deliverables Checklist**

- Officer certificates and bring-down.
- Secretary certificates, good standing, charter/bylaws.
- Third-party consents and payoff letters.
- Legal opinions (if required).
- Escrow agreement and funding confirmations.
- FIRPTA and tax certifications, if applicable.
- Stockholder approval documentation.

**Schedules/Exhibits**

- Disclosure schedules.
- Form of Articles/Certificate of Merger.
- Form of escrow agreement.
- Form of voting/support agreement.
- Form of registration rights agreement.
- Form of employment/retention agreements.

**Clause Templates**

```text
Merger Consideration: At the Effective Time, each Share ... shall be converted into the right to receive [Cash/Stock] equal to [Exchange Ratio], subject to [Escrow/Holdback] and [Adjustment/Earnout].

MAE Definition: "Material Adverse Effect" means ... excluding changes in general economic conditions, industry conditions, changes in law, and other carve-outs [tailor carve-outs and disproportionate effect].

Termination Fee: If this Agreement is terminated under Section [X] due to [superior proposal/board change], then [Party] shall pay [Fee] within [X] days, as liquidated damages and not as a penalty.
```

## Guidelines

- Match the merger procedure to the parties’ formation jurisdictions and charter requirements.
- Align consideration mechanics with financing, escrow, and payment workflows.
- Use disclosure schedules to qualify reps; avoid silent qualifiers in the main text.
- Define MAE and Knowledge with clear carve-outs and standards.
- Track no-shop/go-shop, fiduciary out, and matching rights with precision.
- Include antitrust and other regulatory approvals and timing obligations.
- Treat tax-free reorganization language as conditional unless confirmed.
- Confirm appraisal/dissenters’ rights applicability and process.
- Keep defined terms consistent and avoid duplicated or conflicting provisions.
