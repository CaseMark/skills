# Form 10-Q Quarterly Report

Produce an SEC-compliant quarterly report with required financial and non-financial disclosures.

## Prerequisites

1. Registrant identity details: legal name, EIN, SEC file number, exchange ticker, jurisdiction of incorporation, principal office address, phone.
2. Quarter period dates and filer status classification.
3. Prior Form 10-K and most recent 10-Q for roll-forward disclosures.
4. Unaudited interim financial statements and footnote support under GAAP.
5. Accounting policy updates, estimates, contingencies, and subsequent events through filing date.
6. MD&A variance drivers and segment data, including volume, price, mix, and cost drivers.
7. Liquidity data: cash flows, credit facilities, covenants, and capital plans.
8. Market risk exposures and hedging activities.
9. Disclosure controls evaluation and internal control changes for the quarter.
10. Legal proceedings updates and material risk factor changes.
11. Equity issuances, repurchases, defaults, mine safety data if applicable.
12. Material contracts and exhibit list, SOX certifications, XBRL tag set.

### Item 1 Financial Statements Checklist

- Condensed balance sheet with current quarter end and prior fiscal year end.
- Condensed statements of operations for quarter and YTD with prior-year comparatives.
- Condensed statements of comprehensive income for quarter and YTD with comparatives.
- Condensed statements of cash flows for YTD with prior-year comparative.
- Condensed statements of stockholders' equity.
- Footnotes: significant policies, changes in estimates, contingencies, subsequent events, segment info, EPS and dilution, related-party transactions.

### MD&A Structure (Item 2)

```text
Executive Overview
Results of Operations
Liquidity and Capital Resources
Known Trends, Events, Demands, Commitments, and Uncertainties
Critical Accounting Policies and Estimates (if material changes)
```

### Final Quality Control Checklist

- Cross-foot and foot all financial statements; reconcile to MD&A and notes.
- Verify all cross-references, exhibit numbers, and incorporation-by-reference cites.
- Ensure units and rounding are consistent across the filing.
- Confirm consistency between legal proceedings and contingencies note.
- Confirm risk factor updates reflect only material changes since last filing.
- Confirm certification text matches required SEC form language.
- Confirm EDGAR formatting and XBRL tagging pass validation.

## Guidelines

- Use company-specific, quantified explanations; avoid boilerplate.
- Clearly label materiality thresholds and assumptions; mark uncertain items with `[VERIFY]`.
- If an item is not applicable or has no material update, state that explicitly or omit per form instructions.
- Do not incorporate SOX 906 exhibits into Securities Act registration statements [VERIFY].
- Apply plain English standards and avoid legal jargon; Rule 421(d) of Regulation C [VERIFY].
- Coordinate with finance and legal to ensure disclosures align with GAAP and SEC rules.
