# Subscription Agreement

Draft a negotiated subscription agreement for corporate services or SaaS relationships.

## Prerequisites

1. Parties' legal names, entity types, jurisdictions, addresses, and signatories.
2. Description of subscription services/products, access method, deliverables, SLAs, support tiers, and usage limits.
3. Commercial terms: fees, billing cadence, payment methods, taxes, discounts, credits, and price change policy.
4. Term details: effective date, initial term, renewal mechanics, and termination rights.
5. Data and security posture: data types, DPA needs, security standards, sub-processors, retention.
6. Risk positions: warranty scope, liability cap, indemnities, IP ownership, governing law and venue.

### Clause Requirements Checklist

- Parties/Recitals: entity type, jurisdiction of formation, principal address, purpose of agreement.
- Scope: clear services description, limitations, uptime/SLAs, support hours, change management.
- Term/Renewal: initial term, auto-renewal notice window, non-renewal method, effects of termination.
- Fees: amounts, billing cycle, payment due date, late fees, tax allocation, fee changes.
- IP: provider IP retained; license scope to subscriber; subscriber data ownership.
- Data/Privacy: data types, security measures, breach notice timing, retention/deletion, sub-processors.
- Warranties: authority and compliance; service warranty (if any); disclaimer of implied warranties.
- Liability: cap amount and basis; excluded damages; carve-outs (IP, confidentiality, gross negligence).
- Indemnity: third-party IP claims by provider; misuse or data issues by subscriber; procedure and control.
- Boilerplate: assignment, amendment, waiver, force majeure, severability, entire agreement, counterparts.

### Template Snippets (adapt and integrate)

```text
Auto-Renewal
The Initial Term begins on [Effective Date] and continues for [Term Length]. Unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least [Notice Period] before the end of the then-current term, the Agreement renews for successive [Renewal Term] terms. If required by applicable auto-renewal laws, Provider will deliver renewal reminders and cancellation instructions in the required form.

License Grant
Provider grants Subscriber a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable license during the Term to access and use the Services solely for Subscriber's internal business purposes, subject to the Documentation and this Agreement. All rights not expressly granted are reserved.

Limitation of Liability
Except for Excluded Claims, each party's aggregate liability arising out of or relating to this Agreement will not exceed the fees paid or payable by Subscriber to Provider in the [12] months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party is liable for indirect, special, consequential, punitive, or lost profits damages.

Termination Effects
Upon termination, Subscriber's access to the Services will cease, and Provider will make Subscriber Data available for export for [Export Period]. After that period, Provider may delete Subscriber Data in accordance with its retention policy, unless legally required to retain it.
```

### Consistency Checks

- Defined terms are used consistently and capitalized.
- Fee calculations align with invoicing and payment terms.
- Termination section aligns with data retention and access shutoff.
- Liability cap aligns with indemnity carve-outs and warranty scope.
- Governing law and venue align with notice and dispute sections.

## Guidelines

- Default to B2B posture; if consumer-facing, add consumer protections and update auto-renewal handling.
- Avoid scope ambiguity: tie services to a schedule, order form, or SLA attachment.
- Do not grant implied IP rights; specify license scope, restrictions, and ownership of derivatives.
- Align data terms with actual processing activities; add a DPA if personal data is processed.
- Keep warranty language and disclaimers aligned with jurisdictional limits.
- Use clear notice mechanics and cure periods for termination for cause.
- If electronic signatures are used, ensure compliance with applicable e-signature laws and internal policy.
