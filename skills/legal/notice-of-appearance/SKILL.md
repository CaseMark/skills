# Notice of Appearance of Counsel

Drafts a court-ready Notice of Appearance establishing an attorney's representation on the record, with proper caption, service certificate, and jurisdictional compliance.

## Prerequisites

1. **Case documents** — complaint, answer, or docket sheet with full caption, case number, and judge assignment
2. **Attorney details** — full name, firm, bar number, contact info, e-filing registration
3. **Jurisdiction identified** — federal (FRCP) vs. state vs. specialized tribunal
4. **Appearance type determined** — initial appearance, substitution, or additional counsel
5. **Service list** — all counsel of record and pro se parties with addresses/emails

## Process

### Step 1: Extract Case Information from Documents

Search uploaded documents for:

| Field | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Court name (with district/division) | Caption of complaint or answer |
| Party names (exact spelling) | Caption |
| Case/docket number | Caption or docket sheet |
| Judge assignment | Docket sheet or case management order |
| Existing counsel of record | Docket or prior filings |
| Pending deadlines/standing orders | Case management or scheduling orders |

### Step 2: Determine Jurisdictional Requirements

| Jurisdiction | Key Variations |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Federal** | District + division in caption; FRCP 11 authority; separate federal bar number may be required |
| **State** | Department numbers, case-type codes, category designators in caption vary by state |
| **Pro hac vice** | Reference granting order; identify local co-counsel if required |

Flag if substitution requires a **separate motion** (vs. notice alone) or **court approval** before effective.

### Step 3: Draft the Notice

**Template structure:**

```
[COURT CAPTION — exact jurisdiction formatting]

NOTICE OF APPEARANCE OF COUNSEL

PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that [Attorney Full Name], of [Law Firm Name],
hereby enters an appearance as [counsel of record / additional counsel /
substitute counsel] for [Party Name], [Designation], in the
above-captioned matter, pursuant to [Rule Citation].

[If substitution: "This appearance is filed in substitution for
[Prior Counsel Name], who [has filed a motion to withdraw /
has been granted leave to withdraw by order dated ___]."]

Attorney Information:
  Name:       [Full legal name as registered with bar]
  Firm:       [Complete firm name]
  Address:    [Street address — no P.O. Box unless local rule permits]
  Phone:      [With area code]
  Fax:        [If required by court]
  Email:      [E-service designated address]
  Bar No.:    [State bar number]
  E-File ID:  [If required by local rules]

[Signature Block]

CERTIFICATE OF SERVICE

I hereby certify that on [Date], a true and correct copy of the
foregoing Notice of Appearance of Counsel was served upon all
counsel of record and pro se parties via [method] at the addresses
listed below:

[Service List — Name, Firm, Address, Email for each recipient]

/s/ [Attorney Name]
```

### Step 4: Signature Block Rules

| Filing Method | Signature Format |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Paper | Original ink signature + printed name + bar number below |
| E-filing | `/s/ Attorney Name` or court-specific digital signature convention |

Include attestation of bar admission or pro hac vice status per court requirements.

### Step 5: Pre-Filing Checklist

- [ ] Party names match caption exactly
- [ ] Case number verified against docket
- [ ] Bar number valid for filing jurisdiction
- [ ] E-service email is active and monitored
- [ ] Caption formatting matches local rules (margins, font, spacing)
- [ ] Certificate of service lists all parties/counsel
- [ ] Service method complies with jurisdiction's e-filing rules
- [ ] Filing fee confirmed (most jurisdictions: none; verify)
- [ ] If substitution: withdrawal motion filed or order entered
- [ ] Standing orders and pending deadlines identified and calendared

## Guidelines

- **Caption precision**: Rejection risk if formatting deviates from local rules — always verify against court website or ECF system
- **Multiple parties**: List each represented party separately; flag potential conflicts
- **Corporate entities**: Verify exact legal name against secretary of state records
- **Government parties**: Check for special appearance rules or sovereign immunity considerations
- **Timing**: Entry of appearance subjects attorney to all existing case deadlines immediately
- **Retain conformed copy**: Note filing date/time stamp for case file
- **Do not assume** local rule details — prompt user to confirm jurisdiction-specific formatting if not determinable from documents
