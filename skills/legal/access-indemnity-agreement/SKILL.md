# Access and Indemnity Agreement (Due Diligence)

Draft a short-form, execution-ready right-of-entry contract that allocates operational control and risk during pre-closing inspections.

## Prerequisites

1. Governing law and property location (state-specific anti-indemnity, lien, and environmental rules).
2. Parties and roles: owner, buyer/recipient, property manager, lender (if any), and who administers access.
3. Property details: address/legal description, occupancy/tenants, restricted areas, hazards, union/site rules.
4. Deal posture: PSA/LOI status, diligence period, exclusivity, termination triggers.
5. Scope of investigations: Phase I only vs Phase II, destructive testing, sampling, drones, roof access.
6. Insurance requirements: limits, additional insureds, primary/noncontributory, waiver of subrogation, carrier ratings.
7. Confidentiality framework: existing NDA, report delivery expectations, privacy/security limits.

**Defined Terms Checklist**

- `Property`, `Access Period`, `Investigations`, `Invasive Activities`
- `Owner Indemnitees`, `Recipient Representatives`
- `Confidential Information`, `Work Plan`

**Section List (order)**
1. Parties, Property, Recitals, and **License Grant** (revocable, non-exclusive, not a lease/easement).
2. Access Period and **Access Procedures** (notice, hours, escorts, safety, tenant non-interference).
3. **Permitted vs Invasive Activities** with Work Plan approval gate.
4. **Insurance Requirements** and endorsement delivery.
5. **Indemnity, Defense, and Survival** with discovery carve-out.
6. **Restoration, Lien Control, and Incident Response** with self-help.
7. **Confidentiality and Report Handling** (NDA cross-reference, actual-knowledge trap).
8. **Disclaimers, Termination, Dispute Terms, Priority/Integration**.
9. Signatures.

**Clause Templates (adapt and bracket business terms)**

License Grant
```text
Owner grants Recipient a limited, revocable, non-exclusive license (not a lease or easement) to enter the Property solely to perform the Investigations during the Access Period, subject to Owner’s reasonable rules and tenant rights. Recipient has no possessory interest or exclusive use of the Property.
```

Access Procedures
```text
Entries require at least [48] hours’ prior written notice to [Owner Rep] identifying each entrant, purpose, equipment, and confirmation of current insurance. Owner may require escorts, reschedule for operational reasons, and deny entry for non-compliance. No tenant contact without Owner’s written consent.
```

Permitted vs Invasive Activities
```text
Permitted Investigations include visual inspections, measurements, surveys, and Phase I ESA activities without sampling. Invasive Activities (soil borings, sampling, cores, roof/slab penetrations, system shutdowns) require Owner’s prior written consent based on a Work Plan (scope, schedule, contractor licenses, insurance, restoration plan).
```

Insurance (tie access to evidence of coverage)
```text
As a condition to entry, Recipient and each Recipient Representative shall maintain CGL on an occurrence form with limits not less than [$1,000,000] per occurrence and [$2,000,000] aggregate, workers’ compensation as required by law, and employer’s liability of [$500,000]. If Invasive Activities are approved: umbrella/excess of [$5,000,000] and Contractor’s Pollution Liability of [$1,000,000]. Owner, property manager, and [LENDER] are additional insureds on a primary and noncontributory basis. Deliver certificates and endorsements (CG 20 10 and CG 20 37) [VERIFY] before entry.
```

Indemnity + Discovery Carve-Out
```text
Recipient shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Owner Indemnitees from claims, losses, liens, and costs (including reasonable attorneys’ fees) arising from entry, Investigations, Invasive Activities, or Recipient’s breach, except for the mere discovery of pre-existing conditions not caused or exacerbated by Recipient. Carve out Owner’s [gross negligence/willful misconduct/sole negligence] as required by governing law [VERIFY].
```

Restoration + Lien Control
```text
Recipient shall promptly repair damage and restore to substantially the same condition. If Recipient fails to commence repairs within [5] business days (immediately for safety hazards), Owner may self-help and invoice costs plus [15]% admin fee. Recipient shall keep Property free of liens and discharge or bond any lien within [10] days after notice.
```

Confidentiality + Reports
```text
Confidential Information includes documents, observations, photos, and data derived from access. Disclosure only to Representatives bound by confidentiality. Compelled disclosure requires notice and cooperation. Report delivery: [full reports / factual data only / no reports]; if Owner declines reports, state that Owner is not receiving them to avoid “actual knowledge” consequences [VERIFY].
```

Priority Clause (if PSA exists)
```text
If a PSA exists, its access provisions control only to the extent of an express conflict; insurance, indemnity, restoration, and confidentiality obligations herein survive unless expressly superseded.
```

## Guidelines

- Always characterize access as a **license**, not a lease or easement.
- Require **Work Plan approval** before any invasive testing.
- Tie **entry** to receipt of acceptable **endorsements**, not just certificates.
- Indemnity must cover **defense costs, liens, tenant claims, and property manager/lender**.
- Include a **discovery carve-out** for pre-existing conditions but keep liability for **exacerbation**.
- Use **contractual lien cure periods**; do not claim statutory deadlines without verification.
- If governing law is TX/NY/CA/FL, verify anti-indemnity requirements and formatting rules [VERIFY].
- Environmental reporting obligations are fact- and state-specific; do not state them as mandatory without verification [VERIFY].
- If an NDA exists, specify which document controls confidentiality to avoid conflicts.
- All outputs require attorney review in the property’s jurisdiction.
