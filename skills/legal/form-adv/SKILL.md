# Form ADV Parts 1 and 2

Produce a complete, internally consistent Form ADV (Parts 1A/1B/2A/2B) ready for compliance review and filing.

## Prerequisites

1. Entity formation documents and ownership cap table
2. Current AUM calculations with discretionary and non-discretionary breakdowns
3. Advisory agreements, fee schedules, and billing policies
4. Compliance manual, code of ethics, custody policies, trade allocation, proxy voting policies
5. Affiliations and related-person disclosures
6. Disciplinary history for firm and management persons
7. List of advisory services and client types
8. Supervised person bios for Part 2B
9. Prior Form ADV filings or amendments (if any)
10. Target filing jurisdiction(s) and state-specific requirements

## Output Structure / Process

Deliverables:
- Form ADV Part 1A responses (IARD-ready)
- Form ADV Part 1B (if state-registered)
- Form ADV Part 2A brochure
- Form ADV Part 2B supplements
- Cover page and filing date
- Open Issues list for missing or unverified data

Part 1A checklist:
- Identifying information, form of organization, fiscal year
- Owners and control persons, organizational chart
- Business activities and advisory services
- RAUM calculations and methodology
- Client types and number of clients
- Private funds reporting status, if applicable
- Custody, client asset safeguards, account authority
- Financial industry affiliations and related persons
- Soft dollars, brokerage, principal/agency cross participation
- Disclosure Reporting Pages (DRPs) if any event applies
- Schedules A/B/C as required

Part 1B checklist (state):
- State-specific questions per jurisdiction
- Bonding, net worth, or custody statements if required
- Additional disclosures required by state regulator

Part 2A content requirements:
- Plain-English narrative with consistent terminology
- Conflicts disclosed with mitigation measures
- Fee examples with tiers and billing timing
- Specific risk disclosures tailored to strategies
- Custody and safeguarding explanation
- Solicitor/referral arrangements and compensation
- Proxy voting policy availability

Part 2B checklist (each supervised person):
- Name, title, and business address
- Education and business experience (last 5 years)
- Professional designations and issuing bodies
- Disciplinary events
- Other business activities and additional compensation
- Supervision and contact for questions

Consistency checks:
- RAUM and client counts match Part 1A and Part 2A
- Ownership and control persons align with Schedules
- Fees and services match agreements and marketing materials
- Disciplinary disclosures consistent across Parts 1 and 2
- Custody status consistently described

Open Issues format:
- `TBD` items listed with source needed and due date
- Any regulatory ambiguity tagged for counsel review

## Guidelines

- Use current SEC Form ADV instructions and state guidance; do not rely on outdated filings.
- Do not state “no disciplinary history” without verified sources.
- Avoid legal conclusions; report facts and disclosures as required by the form.
- If thresholds or state requirements are unclear, flag `[VERIFY]` and request confirmation.
- Keep Part 2A plain-English and client-facing; avoid legalese.
- Ensure all figures and dates are consistent across all parts and schedules.
