# Capital Call Notice

Formal notice demanding capital contributions under the governing agreement with precise calculations and payment mechanics.

## Prerequisites

1. Governing agreement(s): LPA/LLC operating agreement, subscription agreements, amendments, side letters.
2. Notice provisions: authorized purposes, notice period, delivery method, cure/default process, governing law.
3. Fund/entity details: legal name, GP/manager name, signatory authority, address, letterhead.
4. Investor schedule: commitments, contributions to date, remaining unfunded, ownership percentage.
5. Capital call purpose: investment or expense description, amount breakdown, compliance with permitted uses.
6. Payment instructions: beneficiary, bank, ABA/SWIFT, account number, reference codes, alternate methods.
7. Contact details for questions and payment confirmation routing.

## Output Structure / Process

**Intake Checklist**

| Item | Required Detail | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Default remedies | Interest rate, dilution/forfeiture, suspension, cure period | Default clause |

**Calculation Statement**

Use one paragraph that states the formula and confirms compliance with the agreement (e.g., pro rata based on commitments unless modified by side letters). Include aggregate amount called and total fund commitment base.

**Delivery Method**

State the delivery method required by the notice clause and confirm delivery address or email on record.

## Guidelines

- Match every citation to the governing agreement; do not paraphrase section numbers.
- Use full month names for dates and specify time zone for the deadline.
- Do not exceed authorized purposes; tie each use to the agreement.
- Reflect side letter modifications only for the applicable investor and keep other terms confidential.
- State remedies exactly as written; include cure periods or required follow-up notices.
- Verify signatory authority and entity names against formation and management docs.
- Include only necessary investment context; avoid disclosing non-public deal terms unless permitted.
- Confirm delivery method and addresses comply with notice provisions and any electronic delivery requirements.
- Mirror the governing law and jurisdiction clause; do not introduce new law terms.
- Keep amounts consistent across letter body and schedule; re-check arithmetic.
