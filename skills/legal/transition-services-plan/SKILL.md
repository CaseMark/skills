# Transition Services Plan

Drafts a Transition Services Plan exhibit to an Asset Purchase Agreement governing post-closing seller support for operational continuity.

## Prerequisites

1. **Executed or near-final Asset Purchase Agreement** — need Closing Date, defined terms, notice provisions, indemnification framework
2. **Functional area inventory** — which departments/operations require transition support
3. **Key personnel lists** — designated contacts from both Seller and Buyer per functional area
4. **Duration parameters** — agreed or proposed support periods per service category
5. **Compensation structure** — whether TSA services are included in Purchase Price or separately compensated

## Output Structure

### Document Header

```
TRANSITION SERVICES PLAN

Exhibit [__] to that certain Asset Purchase Agreement dated [DATE]
by and between [SELLER LEGAL NAME] ("Seller") and [BUYER LEGAL NAME] ("Buyer")

Effective Date: [Closing Date]
Expiration Date: [DATE], subject to extension per Section [__]
```

### Section Outline

| Section | Content |
|---|---|
| **1. Definitions & Interpretation** | Incorporate APA defined terms; define Plan-specific terms |
| **2. Scope of Services** | Functional area matrix (see below) |
| **3. Service Standards** | Consistent with Seller's historical practices; commercially reasonable efforts |
| **4. Compensation** | Included in Purchase Price OR separate fee schedule with rates/payment terms |
| **5. Governance** | Transition coordinators, meeting cadence, escalation path |
| **6. Term & Termination** | Commencement, expiration, early termination on notice, extension mechanics |
| **7. Relationship to APA** | APA controls on conflict; confidentiality/indemnification incorporated; no partnership/employment created |
| **8. Signatures** | Authorized representatives, name/title/date blocks |

### Functional Area Matrix

Draft a table for each service category with these columns:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Service Category** | Functional area name |
| **Scope of Services** | Specific tasks, deliverables, knowledge transfer items |
| **Seller Contact** | Name, title, email/phone |
| **Buyer Contact** | Name, title, email/phone |
| **Duration** | Start date through end date |
| **Milestones/Deliverables** | Measurable completion criteria |

### Required Functional Areas

| Area | Typical Scope | Typical Duration |
|---|---|---|
| **Customer/Client Transition** | Introductions, relationship history, file transfer, ownership communication | 30–60 days |
| **Vendor/Supplier Management** | Account transfer/assignment, pricing history, qualification processes | 45–90 days |
| **Employee/HR Integration** | Onboarding support, personnel files, policy explanation, employee relations guidance | 60–90 days |
| **Financial/Accounting** | Record transfer, book closing for pre-closing periods, banking relationship transition, reconciliations | 60–90 days (min. one full accounting cycle) |
| **IT Systems & Data** | Data migration, software/license transfer, credentials, training, system integration | 60–90+ days |

### Compensation Clause Templates

**If included in Purchase Price:**
> The transition services described herein are included within the Purchase Price set forth in the Asset Purchase Agreement. No additional compensation shall be due to Seller except as set forth in a separate written agreement.

**If separately compensated:**
> Seller shall be compensated for transition services at the rates set forth in Schedule [__]. Buyer shall reimburse Seller for pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses within [30] days of invoice.

### Governance Framework

- [ ] Designate one Transition Coordinator per party
- [ ] Weekly or bi-weekly status meetings
- [ ] Written progress reports against milestones
- [ ] Escalation: Coordinator → Senior Management → dispute resolution per APA
- [ ] Modifications by written agreement only

## Guidelines

- **APA consistency** — cross-check all defined terms, dates, and cross-references against the Asset Purchase Agreement
- **Realistic timelines** — build in extension mechanisms for complex services; avoid rigid deadlines without cure periods
- **Measurable deliverables** — each functional area should have concrete completion criteria, not open-ended obligations
- **Seller burden** — ensure transition obligations do not unreasonably interfere with Seller's retained business operations
- **Confidentiality** — incorporate APA confidentiality provisions by reference; add specific data-handling requirements for IT/HR transfers
- **No agency/employment** — include explicit disclaimer that TSA does not create employment, partnership, or joint venture
- **Expense caps** — if Buyer reimburses expenses, include pre-approval thresholds and caps
- **Jurisdiction** — governed by same law as APA; confirm forum selection clause applies
