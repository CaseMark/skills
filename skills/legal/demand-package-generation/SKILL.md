# Demand Package Generation

Compiles evidence, calculates damages, and generates settlement demand packages.

## Prerequisites

1. **Medical summary** — completed chronology
2. **Billing records** — all medical bills
3. **Lost wages documentation** — employment records, tax returns
4. **Liability evidence** — police reports, photos, witness statements

## Workflow

### 1. Economic Damages
Past medical, future medical, lost wages, lost earning capacity, property damage — with sources for each.

### 2. Non-Economic Damages
Pain/suffering narrative from records. Loss of enjoyment (specific activities). Emotional distress (documented treatment). Compare to jurisdiction verdict ranges.

### 3. Assemble
Demand letter with liability analysis. Medical chronology. Itemized damages. Supporting exhibits. Specific demand with deadline.

### 4. Quality Check
Verify all bills accounted for. Confirm liability facts supported by evidence. Ensure demand amount defensible. Store in vault.

## Pitfalls
- Don't inflate — adjusters spot padding, it kills credibility
- Include all bills — missing a small bill invites scrutiny of the whole package
- Know jurisdiction rules — damage caps, comparative fault, collateral source
- Set realistic response deadlines
