# Parking License Agreement

Drafts a revocable parking license for commercial office tenancies that protects the licensor while clearly avoiding creation of any lease, easement, or bailment.

## Prerequisites

1. **Underlying office lease** — executed or in final form, with term dates, notice clauses, and renewal provisions
2. **Parking facility details** — address, type (garage/surface), number and type of spaces (reserved vs. unreserved), any existing rules & regulations
3. **Party information** — full legal names, entity types, states of formation, principal addresses for both licensor and licensee
4. **Commercial terms** — monthly fee per space, payment method, escalation mechanism, insurance minimums

## Output Structure

Draft a complete Parking License Agreement with the following sections in order:

### 1. Header & Parties

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Title | "PARKING LICENSE AGREEMENT" — centered, caps |
| Effective date | Tied to lease commencement or specified date |
| Licensor | Full legal name, entity type, state, address |
| Licensee | Full legal name, entity type, state, address |
| Recitals | Licensor operates facility; Licensee holds office lease; desires parking privileges |

### 2. Grant of License & Legal Characterization

- Number of spaces, reserved vs. unreserved, specific location within facility
- **Required disclaimers** (include all three):
  - Not a lease, easement, or property interest — revocable license only
  - Not a bailment — no custody/control of vehicles or contents
  - No liability for theft, damage, vandalism, or loss except licensor's gross negligence or willful misconduct

### 3. Term & Lease Linkage

- Co-terminous with underlying office lease — starts and ends with lease
- Auto-terminates on early lease termination (default, casualty, condemnation, mutual agreement) — no separate notice required
- Renewal/extension requires mutual agreement on new terms and pricing
- Cannot extend beyond lease term under any circumstance
- Obligation to remove vehicles immediately upon termination

### 4. Fees & Payment

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Rate | $ per space per month; total monthly obligation |
| Due date | 1st of each month (typical) |
| Method | Check / wire / ACH as specified |
| Late fee | Flat amount or % of monthly fee |
| Interest | Specified rate (within usury limits) |
| Escalation | CPI adjustment, fixed %, or licensor-set with 30–60 day notice |
| Taxes | Specify sales/use tax responsibility |

### 5. Permitted Use & Restrictions

- **Allowed**: Standard passenger vehicles, light trucks, SUVs of licensee's employees, officers, directors, authorized visitors
- **Prohibited**:
  - Commercial vehicles, oversized trucks, RVs, boats, trailers, inoperable vehicles
  - Vehicle repair, maintenance, washing, commercial activity
  - Overnight parking (if applicable) or use outside facility hours
  - Sublicense, assignment, or transfer of parking rights

- All vehicles must be registered, insured, and operable
- Licensee responsible for user compliance

### 6. Rules & Regulations

- Licensee must comply with facility rules as amended by licensor in sole discretion
- Licensor reserves right to:
  - Temporarily close facility for maintenance, repairs, emergencies (reasonable efforts to minimize disruption)
  - Reconfigure facility, relocate unreserved spaces, modify access routes
  - Implement new management systems (gates, apps, validation)
- Licensee responsible for access cards/permits; liable for replacement cost if lost

### 7. Liability, Indemnification & Insurance

**Indemnification**: Licensee indemnifies, defends, and holds harmless licensor and its officers, directors, employees, agents, and affiliates from all claims, damages, costs (including attorneys' fees) arising from licensee's use, acts/omissions of licensee's users, or breach of agreement.

**Insurance minimums** (fill in agreed amounts):

| Coverage | Minimum |
|----------|---------|
| CGL — per occurrence | $[AMOUNT] |
| CGL — aggregate | $[AMOUNT] |
| Auto liability | Covering all vehicles using spaces |

- Licensor named as additional insured
- Licensee's insurance primary and non-contributory
- Certificates of insurance due before first use and annually

### 8. Termination & Default

**Licensor termination rights:**

| Trigger | Notice/Cure |
|---------|-------------|
| Non-payment | [X] days written notice to cure |
| Other breach | [X] days written notice to cure |
| Underlying lease terminated | Immediate, no notice required |
| Without cause | [X] days prior written notice |

**Post-termination obligations:**
- Cease use immediately; remove all vehicles; return access devices
- Pre-termination obligations survive (including indemnification)
- **Holdover**: Double monthly fee (daily pro-rated basis); licensor may tow at licensee's expense

### 9. Assignment & Transfer

- License is personal to licensee — no assignment, sublicense, or transfer without licensor's prior written consent (sole and absolute discretion)
- Unauthorized transfer is void and constitutes material breach
- Even consented assignment does not release licensee unless licensor expressly agrees in writing
- Licensee liable for acts/omissions of any permitted assignee

### 10. General Provisions

Include standard boilerplate:
- Entire agreement / merger clause
- Amendment requires signed writing
- Severability
- Governing law (state where facility is located, no conflicts-of-law)
- Binding on successors and permitted assigns
- Notices (personal delivery, certified mail, overnight courier, email) with addresses
- No waiver of breach constitutes waiver of subsequent breach
- No partnership, joint venture, or agency created
- Prevailing party recovers reasonable attorneys' fees and costs

### 11. Signature Block

Separate blocks for Licensor and Licensee:
```
LICENSOR:
[ENTITY NAME]

By: ___________________________
Name: _________________________
Title: ________________________
Date: _________________________

LICENSEE:
[ENTITY NAME]

By: ___________________________
Name: _________________________
Title: ________________________
Date: _________________________
```

Signature page must share at least one substantive provision — never a standalone page. Add witness/notary lines only if required by local law.

## Guidelines

- This is a **license**, not a lease — reinforce this characterization throughout; avoid lease terminology ("tenant," "landlord," "demised premises")
- Use "licensor" and "licensee" consistently
- Liability cap at gross negligence / willful misconduct is standard — do not expand licensor exposure without explicit instruction
- Escalation clauses must comply with the underlying lease's parking fee provisions if any exist
- Insurance minimums should match or exceed those required under the office lease
- Confirm local jurisdiction requirements for notarization or witnesses before including
- If spaces are reserved, describe by number, level, or map reference — attach an exhibit if needed
- All dollar amounts and cure periods are bracketed placeholders — confirm with client before finalizing
