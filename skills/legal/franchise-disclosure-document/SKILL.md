# Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD)

Draft a complete FTC Rule–compliant FDD with state addenda and exhibits, aligned to the franchise agreement.

## Prerequisites

1. **Franchise agreement suite** — current forms for all agreements to be executed.
2. **Corporate profile** — legal entity, ownership, affiliates, officers/directors bios, addresses.
3. **Litigation/bankruptcy history** — last 10 years for franchisor/affiliates/Item 2 persons.
4. **Financials** — audited statements (up to 3 years) and auditor report.
5. **Fee schedules** — initial, ongoing, and contingent fees with formulas and timing.
6. **Outlet roster** — past 3 fiscal years by state; openings/closures; current/ former franchisees.
7. **IP portfolio** — trademarks, registrations, and any disputes.
8. **Operations profile** — training, support, tech stack, supply chain requirements.
9. **State targets** — states where offering will be made; registration needs [VERIFY].

**B. Front Matter**

- Title block: “FRANCHISE DISCLOSURE DOCUMENT” with legal name, entity type, state of formation, principal address, effective date.
- FTC cover page cautionary statement: use FTC-required text verbatim per 16 C.F.R. Part 436 (do not paraphrase).
- State law notice and agency contact direction (as required).
- Table of contents listing Items 1–23 and all exhibits with page numbers.

**E. Item 19 Controls**

- If no FPR: insert FTC‑required negative disclosure statement verbatim.
- If FPR: include data set description, period covered, sample size, assumptions, and cautionary statements; keep substantiation file.

**F. Exhibits**

Required exhibits list (lettered, referenced in TOC and Item 23 receipt)
- Exhibit A: Franchise Agreement (final form)
- Exhibit B: Audited Financial Statements (Item 21)
- Exhibit C: Franchisee List (Item 20)
- Additional: area development, leases, equipment leases, loan documents, guaranty, confidentiality/NDA, non‑compete, ops manual TOC, state addenda, escrow/surety documents (if required)

**H. Quality Control Checklist**

- Item order 1–23 correct and complete.
- All negative disclosures included where applicable.
- All fees and territory terms match agreement text.
- Item 17 and Item 9 tables cross‑reference correct sections.
- TOC and page numbers accurate.
- Exhibits and receipt page list match actual attachments.
- Financial statements current as of effective date; update if outside allowed recency window [VERIFY].
- State addenda cross‑references included in main FDD.

## Guidelines

- Use FTC‑required statements verbatim where mandated; do not paraphrase.
- No marketing language; disclosures must be factual and neutral.
- Do not include FPRs outside Item 19; ensure substantiation for any FPR.
- Align every disclosure with the executed agreement forms; resolve conflicts before final.
- Flag uncertain state‑law requirements with `[VERIFY]` and confirm before issuance.
- Preserve franchisee contact data privacy handling per applicable state rules.
