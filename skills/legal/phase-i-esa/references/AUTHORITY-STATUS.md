## Notes on use

- The 2024-07-08 CERCLA designation of PFOA/PFOS materially affects Phase I REC analysis at properties with historical PFAS-relevant uses. Pre-2024 reports do not reflect this; updated reports for the same property may classify previously-non-REC conditions as RECs.
- E1527-21's emerging-contaminants language is broad enough to reach PFAS without a separate ASTM update; some jurisdictions (e.g., Massachusetts MCP) require explicit PFAS evaluation under state cleanup law independent of ASTM scope.
