# Royalty Deed

Conveys a royalty interest in oil, gas, and other minerals for specified lands with recording-ready terms.

## Prerequisites

1. State and county where the land is located, plus current recording format requirements.
2. Vesting deed or title commitment and the full legal description for the conveyed lands.
3. Interest definition: fixed or floating royalty, fraction/percentage, NPRI vs participating, substances covered, depth/formation limits.
4. Lease posture: existing leases, pooling/unit orders, and whether interest burdens present leases, future leases, or both.
5. Royalty valuation basis: cost-free vs post-production cost deductions, wellhead vs downstream.
6. Parties with full legal names, capacity, and execution authority; marital status if homestead or community property.
7. Warranty level: general, special, or quitclaim; known encumbrances and reservations.
8. Effective date, consideration, and return/preparer information for recording.

## Output Structure / Process

**Input Matrix**

| Field | Required | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jurisdiction (state/county) | Yes | Drives acknowledgment form and recording format. |
| Grantor / Grantee | Yes | Include entity type and capacity. |
| Fraction/percentage | Yes | Specify fixed or floating royalty and base. |
| Lands description | Yes | Attach as Exhibit A if lengthy. |
| Existing leases | Usually | Identify by date/parties/recording data if known. |
| Royalty basis | Yes | Cost-free vs net proceeds. |
| Substance scope | Yes | Oil, gas, other minerals, or limited list. |
| Depth/formation | Optional | Add if limited. |
| Warranty type | Yes | General, special, or quitclaim. |
| Encumbrances/reservations | Yes | “Subject to” clause with list. |
| Pooling/unitization | Optional | If permitted, define allocation. |

**Required Sections**

| Section | Must Include | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Caption/Recording Block | Preparer/return info, space for clerk | Follow state format rules. |
| Parties/Consideration | Full names, addresses, consideration | Include “good and valuable consideration” if needed. |
| Granting Clause | “Grant, bargain, sell, convey” or state-specific verb | Identify royalty interest and base. |
| Land Description | Legal description, county/state | Incorporate Exhibit A if used. |
| Nature of Interest | NPRI or participating; fixed vs floating | Define base royalty and lease burden. |
| Lease/Pooling Provisions | Present and future leases; pooling effect | Clarify proportionate reduction. |
| Royalty Valuation | Cost-free or net proceeds | Define post-production cost treatment. |
| Reservations/Exceptions | Any reserved minerals or rights | Keep consistent with title. |
| Warranty | General/special/quitclaim | Tie to consideration. |
| After-Acquired Title | Optional | If used, apply to royalty only. |
| Execution/Acknowledgment | Signatures, notary | State-specific acknowledgment. |

**Core Clause Templates**

```text
GRANTING CLAUSE
Grantor, for and in consideration of [Consideration], grants and conveys to Grantee
an [undivided X% / X/X] royalty interest in and to all oil, gas, and other minerals
produced and saved from the Lands described in Exhibit A, together with the right
to receive and collect the royalty therefrom, [subject to the terms below].

NATURE OF INTEREST (NPRI)
The interest conveyed is a perpetual, non-participating royalty interest.
Grantee has no right to execute leases, no right to bonus or delay rentals,
and no right to pool or unitize the Lands, except as expressly provided herein.

ROYALTY BASE (FIXED OR FLOATING)
Fixed: The interest is a fixed [X%] of production, regardless of the royalty
reserved in any lease.
Floating: The interest is [X%] of the royalty reserved in present and future leases.

LEASES AND FUTURE LEASES
This conveyance applies to royalties payable under existing leases described as:
[Lease list], and to royalties reserved under future leases covering the Lands.

POOLING / UNITIZATION (OPTIONAL)
If the Lands are pooled/unitized, the royalty shall be proportionately reduced
to the ratio that the Lands bear to the unit acreage.

ROYALTY VALUATION
Cost-free: Royalty shall be free and clear of all costs of production, gathering,
treating, compression, processing, and transportation.
Net proceeds: Royalty shall be based on proceeds received at the point of sale
after deduction of post-production costs [define].

WARRANTY
[General / Special / Quitclaim] warranty of title, subject to the exceptions listed.
```

**Quality Control Checklist**

- Confirm the fraction is a royalty interest, not a mineral interest.
- Verify fixed vs floating language matches the deal.
- Ensure land description matches the vesting deed or title commitment.
- If existing leases exist, specify them and clarify whether interest burdens present and future leases.
- Align cost-free vs net proceeds language with jurisdictional rules and deal terms.
- Confirm any depth/formation limitation is clear and consistent with the lands.
- Confirm “subject to” exceptions match actual encumbrances of record.
- Confirm execution authority and spouse joinder where required.
- Use the correct state acknowledgment form and recording formatting.

## Guidelines

- Use state-specific conveyancing verbs and acknowledgment forms; do not rely on generic forms where a statute mandates wording.
- Avoid ambiguity between a royalty interest and a mineral interest; define the royalty base explicitly.
- Do not include leasing/executive rights in an NPRI unless expressly negotiated.
- If using floating royalty, tie it to the lessor’s royalty reserved in leases.
- If cost deductions are allowed, specify which costs and the valuation point.
- If the land is homestead or community property, address required joinder or consents.
