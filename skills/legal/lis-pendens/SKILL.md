# Lis Pendens (Notice of Pending Action)

Drafts a recordable notice that provides constructive notice of pending litigation affecting title or right to possession.

## Prerequisites

1. **Jurisdiction + county recorder** — state, county, and local recorder formatting rules.
2. **Filed pleading** — complaint or amended complaint that asserts a qualifying real property claim.
3. **Case metadata** — court name, division, case number, filing date, party names.
4. **Property records** — current deed/title report with full legal description and APN/tax ID.

## Guidelines

- Verify real property claim qualifies under controlling statute; do not record for purely monetary or unrelated claims.
- Use deed/title report language verbatim for legal description; street address alone is insufficient.
- Match party names and case number exactly to the operative complaint.
- Insert statutory warnings verbatim where required; mark uncertain text with `[VERIFY]`.
- Confirm county recorder margins, font size, and stamp-space rules before finalizing.
- Ensure service is completed within statutory timeframes; include proof of service when required.
- Flag any jurisdiction-specific expungement standards or probable validity requirements.
