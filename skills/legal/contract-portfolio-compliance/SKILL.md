# Contract Portfolio Compliance

Scans contract portfolios for compliance with regulatory requirements or internal standards.

## Prerequisites

1. **Portfolio** — active contracts in vault
2. **Standard** — regulation/policy to check against (e.g., GDPR, new mandate, updated policy)
3. **Remediation authority** — who approves amendments or waivers

## Workflow

### 1. Define Criteria
Translate regulation into contractual requirements. Create checklist of required, recommended, and prohibited terms.

### 2. Analyze
Per contract: check for required clauses, flag prohibited terms, score (compliant/partial/non-compliant), extract relevant text.

### 3. Prioritize
- **Urgent** — high-value with prohibited terms or missing critical clauses
- **High** — up for renewal soon
- **Medium** — minor updates needed
- **Low** — low-value or expiring soon

### 4. Report
Dashboard: % compliant by category. Non-compliant list with gaps. Remediation plan. Template amendment language.

## Pitfalls
- Prioritize by value and risk, not alphabetically
- Check if regulation has grandfathering/transition provisions
- Sometimes cheaper to wait for renewal than force an amendment
- Compliance report without remediation tracking is just documentation of failure
