# TRID Tolerance Reference

Classifies fees into tolerance buckets, supports variance calculation, validates changed circumstances, and computes cure amounts for CD-vs-LE compliance review.

## Prerequisites

1. Loan Estimate (original and any revised copies, with dates)
2. Closing Disclosure (final or proposed)
3. Creditor's written list of service providers (to classify shopped vs. unshoppped services)
4. Documentation of any changed circumstances

---

## Tolerance Categories

### Zero Tolerance — Cannot Increase (0%)

Any increase requires a valid changed circumstance and revised LE.

| Category | Fee Examples |
|---|---|
| **Creditor/broker fees** | Origination, application, underwriting, processing, discount points, commitment, rate lock |
| **Affiliate fees** | Any fee paid to lender/broker affiliate (title, appraisal, etc.) |
| **Transfer taxes** | State/local transfer tax, mansion tax, documentary stamps |
| **Services borrower cannot shop for** | Appraisal (creditor-selected), credit report, flood determination, tax monitoring |

### 10% Cumulative Tolerance

Measured in aggregate — the **total** of all 10% items on CD cannot exceed the **total** on LE by more than 10%.

| Category | Fee Examples |
|---|---|
| **Recording fees** | Deed recording, mortgage/deed of trust recording |
| **Shopped services (from lender's list)** | Lender's title policy, settlement/closing fee, title search, survey, notary, pest inspection, attorney fees |

> If borrower chose a provider **not on the lender's written list**, that fee shifts to **unlimited tolerance**.

### Unlimited Tolerance

May change freely; original LE must have been made in good faith.

- Prepaid interest (per diem)
- Homeowner's, flood, hazard, and MI premiums
- Initial escrow deposits (taxes, insurance, MI, aggregate adjustment)
- Property costs not required by creditor (HOA dues, assessments, prepaid taxes)
- Services shopped for using a provider **not** on lender's list
- Optional services (home warranty, optional owner's title, home inspection if not lender-required)

## Changed Circumstances Permitting Revised LE

**Deadline:** Revised LE must be delivered within **3 business days** of learning of the change and **at least 4 business days** before consummation. (12 CFR § 1026.19(e)(3)(iv))

| Type | Examples | Documentation |
|---|---|---|
| Extraordinary event | Natural disaster, war affecting property/title | Government declarations, news reports |
| Information inaccuracy | Income/assets differ from stated; property characteristics differ | Updated application, appraisal |
| Previously unavailable information | Title defect, survey encroachment discovered | Title commitment, survey report |
| Borrower-requested change | Different loan product, property, or borrower added | Written request or new application |
| Rate lock | Borrower locks after floating | Rate lock confirmation |
| LE expiration | Closing delayed past 10-business-day expiration | Timeline documentation |

**Invalid reasons:** original underestimate, creditor error, general market movement, creditor-caused delays. Only fees **affected** by the change may be revised.

---

## Cure Requirements

**Deadline:** Within **60 calendar days** after consummation. (12 CFR § 1026.19(f)(2)(v))

```
Zero Tolerance Cure:
  CD Total − LE Total =                 $________

10% Tolerance Cure:
  CD Total − (LE Total × 110%) =        $________  (only if positive)

TOTAL CURE REQUIRED:                    $________

Consummation date:  __/__/____
60-day cure deadline: __/__/____
```

Cure methods: refund check to borrower, credit to escrow, credit to principal.
Provide corrected CD reflecting the cure; retain documentation in loan file.

## Common Errors

| Error | Correct Approach |
|---|---|
| Measuring 10% items individually | Measure as cumulative total |
| Applying 10% tolerance to affiliate fees | Affiliate fees = zero tolerance |
| Revising LE to correct original underestimate | Not a valid changed circumstance |
| Issuing revised LE more than 3 business days after learning of change | Must issue within 3 business days |
| Using changed circumstance to increase unrelated fees | Only revise fees affected by the change |
| Failing to cure within 60 days | Calendar deadline at consummation |

---

## Special Situations

- **Seller credits:** Do not affect tolerance; compare gross fees before credits.
- **Specific lender credits:** Tied to a fee → reduce that fee for tolerance. General lender credits → no effect on individual fee tolerances.
- **Construction loans:** Separate tolerances for construction and permanent phases.
- **Simultaneous subordinate financing:** Each loan has independent tolerances; fees cannot be shifted between loans.
