# Dividend Declaration Resolution

Drafts a board-adopted resolution authorizing a dividend distribution that functions as a binding corporate record and evidentiary foundation for fiduciary compliance.

## Prerequisites

1. **Articles of incorporation** — exact legal name, authorized stock classes, preferred dividend preferences and cumulation rights
2. **Bylaws** — quorum requirements, notice provisions, officer authority for special meetings
3. **Recent financial statements** — balance sheet and income statement dated within 90 days; retained earnings balance
4. **Loan agreements / covenants** — any dividend restrictions, financial maintenance covenants, or lender consent requirements
5. **Prior resolutions** (if any) — for consistency in format and confirmation of accumulated preferred dividends paid
6. **Dividend parameters** — type (cash/stock/property), amount per share, proposed record date, proposed payment date

## Output Structure

### 1. Header

```
[CORPORATION LEGAL NAME]
RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
AUTHORIZING DECLARATION OF [CASH/STOCK/PROPERTY] DIVIDEND

Meeting Type:    [Regular / Special] Meeting of the Board of Directors
Date:            [Full Date]
Location/Remote: [Physical address or virtual platform]
Time:            [HH:MM AM/PM]
```

### 2. Meeting Mechanics Block

| Item | Required Content |
|---|---|
| Notice | Confirm proper notice per bylaw § ___ (special meetings); waiver if applicable |
| Quorum | Confirm quorum present per bylaw § ___ and [State] Corp. Law § ___ |
| Directors present | Full names listed |
| Directors absent | Names + whether excused |
| Presiding officer | Name and title |

---

### 3. WHEREAS Recitals

Draft numbered recitals establishing the legal foundation in this order:

1. **Financial review** — Board reviewed balance sheet and income statement as of [date]; retained earnings of $[X]
2. **Statutory authority** — Dividend declared pursuant to [Del. DGCL §170 / [State] [Cite]] [VERIFY for non-DE jurisdictions]
3. **Equity solvency** — Corporation able to pay debts as they become due in ordinary course
4. **Balance sheet solvency** — Assets exceed liabilities after giving effect to dividend
5. **Business judgment** — Board determined dividend is in the best interests of the corporation considering capital needs and business prospects
6. **Preferred satisfaction** — All accumulated preferred dividends [have been paid / are current] [omit if no preferred outstanding]
7. **Third-party compliance** — [Dividend complies with all loan covenant restrictions / Lender consent obtained dated ___] [omit if N/A]

---

### 4. RESOLVED Clauses

**RESOLVED 1 — Dividend Declaration**
> Declare a [cash] dividend of $[0.00] per share ([written amount] dollars and [XX] cents), on [class] stock, par value $[X.XX] per share, totaling approximately $[aggregate amount] based on [X,XXX,XXX] shares outstanding.

**RESOLVED 2 — Critical Dates**

| Date | Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Declaration Date | Date of this meeting | Board approval date |
| Record Date | [10–30 days post-declaration] | Holders of record entitled to dividend |
| Payment Date | [2–4 weeks post-record date] | Actual distribution date |

**RESOLVED 3 — Eligible Shares**
> Dividend payable to holders of record of [Class] shares as of the Record Date; treasury shares and shares subject to forfeiture are expressly excluded.

**RESOLVED 4 — Officer Authorization**
> CEO, CFO, and Corporate Secretary are authorized and directed to: (a) instruct transfer agent to process and distribute the dividend; (b) make all required accounting entries; (c) prepare and file regulatory notifications; (d) execute any documents necessary to implement distribution. Officers may make ministerial changes that do not alter material terms.

### 5. Vote Record

```
IN FAVOR:    [X] directors — [Names]
AGAINST:     [X] directors — [Names]
ABSTAINING:  [X] directors — [Names, with conflict disclosure if applicable]
```

Document any conflict-of-interest disclosures and recusals by name.

## Guidelines

- **Solvency tests are mandatory** — both equity and balance sheet tests must be addressed in recitals; omitting either creates fiduciary exposure
- **Delaware §170 [VERIFY]** — applies to DE-incorporated entities; confirm equivalent statute for other jurisdictions before citing
- **Preferred stock priority** — verify articles of incorporation for cumulative vs. non-cumulative preferred; accumulated arrears must be cleared before common dividend if cumulative
- **Lender covenants** — check all credit agreements for dividend blockers, basket limitations, and payment conditions before finalizing dates
- **Record date window** — Delaware requires record date be no more than 60 days before payment date [VERIFY]; NYSE/Nasdaq listed companies have additional exchange notice requirements
- **Treasury shares** — must be excluded from dividend eligibility and aggregate calculation
- **Stock dividends** — may require shareholder approval if they alter capital structure; confirm under governing state law and articles
- **Retention** — file executed resolution in corporate minute book; retain per applicable state record-keeping statute
