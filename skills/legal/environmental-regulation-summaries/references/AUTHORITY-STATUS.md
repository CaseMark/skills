## Notes on use

- **Penalty amounts:** Always cite 40 CFR § 19.4 in deliverables; do not embed specific dollar amounts that will date.
- **Post-Sackett WOTUS:** Wetland jurisdictional determinations remain in flux; budget for delays at the Corps and watch for further circuit-level decisions narrowing or clarifying jurisdiction.
- **NEPA without binding CEQ rules:** Practitioners must consult lead-agency NEPA implementing procedures (e.g., 7 CFR Part 1b for USDA; 10 CFR Part 1021 for DOE; 43 CFR Part 46 for DOI; 23 CFR Part 771 for FHWA). Many of these are themselves being revised in 2025–2026; verify current version at draft time.
