# Notice of Assets and Request for Claims

Drafts a probate notice that formally notifies creditors of a decedent's estate and establishes the legal framework for claim submission under applicable state law.

## Prerequisites

1. **Decedent information** — full legal name, aliases, DOD, DOB, last known residence
2. **Personal representative details** — name, relationship to decedent, appointment date, independent administration authority (Y/N)
3. **Court information** — case number, county, state, court address (including department/division)
4. **Counsel information** — attorney name, bar number, firm, address, phone, email
5. **Jurisdiction's probate code** — research the specific state's notice requirements before drafting

## Jurisdictional Research Checklist

Before drafting, verify the following for the applicable state:

| Item | What to Confirm |
|---|---|
| Statutory deadline trigger | First publication, mailing, or date of death? |
| Claim period length | Typically 3–6 months; varies by state |
| Mandatory notice language | Some states require verbatim statutory warnings |
| Asset disclosure requirements | Category-level vs. itemized; estimated value required? |
| Publication requirements | Newspaper of general circulation, legal journal, frequency |
| Service methods | Personal delivery, certified mail, other authorized means |
| Claim form requirements | Court-provided form required or freeform acceptable? |
| Notarization | Required for publication or filing? |
| Filing fees for creditors | Confirm (typically none) |

## Output Structure

### 1. Header

- Title: **NOTICE OF ASSETS AND REQUEST FOR CLAIMS**
- Court jurisdiction (county, state)
- Estate case number
- Date of issuance
- Format per local court rules

### 2. Identification Block

```
Decedent:          [Full Legal Name] aka [Aliases]
Date of Death:     [MM/DD/YYYY]
Last Residence:    [Address, City, County, State]
Personal Rep:      [Name], [Title], appointed [Date]
Authority:         [Independent/Dependent] Administration
Counsel:           [Name] (Bar No. [#]), [Firm]
                   [Address] | [Phone] | [Email]
```

### 3. Asset Disclosure

- Describe assets **by category only** — do not itemize:
  - Real property interests in [County], [State]
  - Financial accounts and investment holdings
  - Personal property and household effects
  - Potential claims, causes of action, or intangible assets
- If jurisdiction requires estimated value, qualify as preliminary and subject to revision
- Include language that additional assets may be discovered

### 4. Claim Requirements and Deadline

Must include all of the following:

| Element | Content |
|---|---|
| **Deadline** | Both calendar date AND descriptive period (e.g., "within four months from first publication, i.e., [Date]") |
| **Bar language** | Claims not timely filed are **barred forever** regardless of validity or lack of actual knowledge |
| **Scope** | Bar applies to contract, tort, statutory, and all other claims unless specifically exempted |
| **Claim contents** | Written; state basis and amount; attach supporting documentation; signed under penalty of perjury |
| **Identifiers** | Must reference decedent's name and estate case number |
| **Special claims** | Address contingent, unliquidated, and pending-litigation claims with any modified procedures |

### 5. Filing and Service Instructions

Provide step-by-step:

1. **File** with the court at: [Full address, department/division]
2. **Serve** a copy on the personal representative or counsel at: [Full address]
3. **Acceptable service methods**: [Per jurisdiction — personal delivery, certified mail, etc.]
4. **Claim forms**: [Where to obtain if required — clerk's office, court website URL]
5. Note: Filing with the court alone is **insufficient** — service on the representative is also required

### 6. Execution Block

```
I declare under penalty of perjury under the laws of the State of [State]
that the foregoing is true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief.

Dated: _______________

_______________________________
[Name], [Title]
Personal Representative of the
Estate of [Decedent Name]
```

If jurisdiction requires notarization, append:

```
State of [State]
County of [County]

Subscribed and sworn before me on [Date].

_______________________________
Notary Public
Name: _______________
Commission No.: _______________
My Commission Expires: _______________
[SEAL]
```

## Formatting Requirements

- 12-point serif font (Times New Roman or equivalent)
- 1-inch margins
- Line spacing per local court rules
- Clear section headings
- Formal tone, but accessible to non-lawyers (creditors include individuals and small vendors)

## Guidelines

- **Calculate deadlines precisely** — errors in the bar date can invalidate the notice and expose the estate to liability
- **Do not over-disclose assets** — category descriptions satisfy notice requirements without compromising negotiating position
- **Verify all statutory citations** against current code; mark uncertain references with `[VERIFY]`
- **Confirm publication requirements** — many jurisdictions require publication in a newspaper of general circulation for a set number of consecutive weeks
- **Due process**: Notice must be sufficiently prominent and unambiguous to withstand constitutional challenge
- **If any required information is missing**, flag it with `[TO BE PROVIDED]` rather than fabricating details
- Document should be **ready for immediate filing and publication** without further revision
