# Due Diligence Document Review

Reviews large document sets to identify risks and material issues for transactions.

## Prerequisites

1. **Document set** — data room or production documents in vault
2. **Review protocol** — what to look for (contracts, litigation, IP, regulatory)
3. **Priority matrix** — which document types matter most

## Workflow

### 1. Inventory
Catalog by type: contracts, filings, correspondence, financials, IP. OCR scanned docs. Flag missing categories.

### 2. Review
Extract per category: key obligations, deadlines, change of control restrictions, unusual terms, pending litigation, compliance status.

### 3. Flag Risks
- **Critical** — undisclosed litigation, material breaches, regulatory violations
- **High** — unfavorable contracts, IP gaps
- **Medium** — approaching deadlines, unusual terms
- **Low** — administrative gaps

### 4. Report
Executive summary, category-by-category findings, risk matrix, missing documents list, recommended follow-ups.

## Pitfalls
- Don't skip boilerplate — risks hide in standard clauses
- Cross-reference — contracts may reference unlisted side letters
- Missing documents are findings, not gaps
- Check whether documents are current or superseded
