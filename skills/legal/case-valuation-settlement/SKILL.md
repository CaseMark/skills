# Case Valuation & Settlement Analysis

Produces data-driven case valuations and settlement recommendations.

## Prerequisites

1. **Case facts** — liability assessment, comparative fault
2. **Medical records** — summarization with severity assessment
3. **Damages calculation** — economic and non-economic
4. **Comparable data** — verdicts and settlements in jurisdiction

## Workflow

### 1. Liability
Strength: clear (90%+), strong (70-90%), contested (50-70%), weak (<50%). Comparative fault %. Coverage limits.

### 2. Severity
Injury type/permanence, treatment duration, daily life impact, future needs.

### 3. Comparables
Search verdict databases. Adjust for venue, economics, demographics. Identify 25th-75th percentile range.

### 4. Valuation
Settlement range (low/mid/high). Policy limits analysis. Risk-adjusted value (trial risk + costs). Settle vs. litigate recommendation.

## Pitfalls
- Policy limits create practical caps regardless of case value
- Same case worth 3x more in one county vs. another
- Factor litigation costs — $500K case costing $200K to try nets less than $350K settlement
- Reassess at key milestones as discovery progresses
