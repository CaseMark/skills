# Petition for Guardianship

Drafts a court-ready petition establishing legal guardianship over an incapacitated adult or minor, tailored to the filing jurisdiction's statutory framework.

## Prerequisites

1. **Jurisdiction identified** — state and county of filing; confirm correct court division (probate, family, surrogate's)
2. **Party information** — full legal names, DOBs, addresses, and relationship between petitioner and proposed ward
3. **Medical evidence** — physician certificates, capacity evaluations, functional assessments, or diagnoses documenting incapacity
4. **Financial information** — ward's assets, income, liabilities (if estate guardianship requested)
5. **Prior legal instruments** — existing POAs, advance directives, representative payee arrangements, or prior guardianship orders
6. **Interested parties list** — names and addresses of all persons entitled to notice (spouse, parents, adult children, siblings, VA, state agency)

## Output Structure

### 1. Caption & Case Header

Format caption per local court rules:

| Element | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Court name | Full name including division/department |
| Case number | Leave blank or use assigned number |
| Case title | "In the Matter of [Ward Full Name], An Alleged Incapacitated Person" (or jurisdiction's convention) |
| Petitioner block | Name, address, relationship, attorney info |
| Document title | "PETITION FOR APPOINTMENT OF GUARDIAN [OF THE PERSON] [AND ESTATE]" |

### 2. Jurisdictional & Venue Allegations

- Statutory authority for filing (cite state guardianship statute)
- Ward's domicile/residence establishing venue
- Court's subject-matter jurisdiction

### 3. Petitioner's Standing

- Relationship to proposed ward
- Basis of knowledge of ward's condition
- Steps taken before seeking guardianship
- Qualifications to serve (or nomination of alternative guardian)
- Disclosure of any conflicts of interest

### 4. Proposed Ward's Circumstances

| Category | Required Detail |
|---|---|
| Personal | Age, living situation, daily care needs, social support |
| Medical | Diagnoses, functional limitations, cognitive status (reference attached physician certificate) |
| Financial | Assets, income sources, liabilities, current management (if estate guardianship sought) |
| For minors | Parental status — absence, incapacity, unfitness, or death; why guardianship exceeds normal custody |

### 5. Legal Grounds & Incapacity Allegations

- Cite applicable statutory definition of incapacity
- Allege specific functional deficits with supporting facts:
  - [ ] Cannot manage personal health/safety
  - [ ] Cannot make informed medical decisions
  - [ ] Cannot manage financial affairs
  - [ ] At risk of exploitation, abuse, or neglect
- Use person-first language; focus on functional impairments, not diagnostic labels

### 6. Least Restrictive Alternative Analysis

Address why each less-restrictive option is insufficient:

| Alternative | Why Insufficient |
|---|---|
| Power of attorney | Ward lacks capacity to execute; existing POA revoked/inadequate |
| Representative payee | Does not cover personal/medical decisions |
| Supported decision-making | Ward's impairments exceed supported framework |
| Voluntary services | Ward unable to consent or cooperate |

### 7. Scope of Authority Requested

Specify guardianship type and enumerate requested powers:

**Guardianship of the Person:**
- [ ] Medical decision-making
- [ ] Residential placement
- [ ] Access to confidential records
- [ ] Consent to treatment / refuse treatment
- [ ] Social and recreational decisions

**Guardianship of the Estate:**
- [ ] Manage income and assets
- [ ] Pay debts and obligations
- [ ] Enter contracts on ward's behalf
- [ ] Manage real property

**Limited guardianship** — identify retained rights explicitly. Tailor powers narrowly to documented deficits.

### 8. Guardian Nomination & Qualifications

- Proposed guardian's full name, address, relationship
- Relevant experience, training, availability
- Criminal background / conflict disclosures as required by jurisdiction
- If professional/public guardian: explain why no suitable family member available
- Consent of proposed guardian (attach)

### 9. Notice & Interested Parties

- List all persons entitled to statutory notice with names and addresses
- Identify any person whose whereabouts are unknown (request alternative service)
- State compliance with any mandatory pre-filing notice requirements

### 10. Exhibits & Attachments Checklist

- [ ] Physician certificate / medical affidavit of incapacity
- [ ] Proposed guardian's consent and disclosure
- [ ] Financial inventory (if estate guardianship)
- [ ] Proposed order / letters of guardianship
- [ ] Notice list with addresses
- [ ] Filing fee or fee waiver
- [ ] Any jurisdiction-specific mandatory forms

### 11. Verification & Signature Block

- Verification clause: petitioner swears under penalty of perjury
- Signature lines for petitioner and attorney
- Notarization block if required by local rules
- Attorney bar number and contact information

## Guidelines

- **Jurisdiction controls** — guardianship statutes vary significantly by state; always confirm: (a) statutory incapacity standard, (b) required medical evidence format, (c) mandatory investigation/visitor requirements, (d) notice and waiting periods, (e) bond requirements
- **Numbered paragraphs** — use consecutively numbered allegations for easy court reference
- **Evidentiary support** — every factual allegation must tie to an identified document or exhibit; no conclusory statements
- **Respectful tone** — guardianship restricts fundamental liberty; the petition must demonstrate genuine necessity
- **Clear-and-convincing standard** — most jurisdictions require this elevated burden; draft allegations accordingly
- **Bond and reporting** — note any bond requirement for estate guardianship and ongoing reporting obligations
- **Emergency/temporary guardianship** — if circumstances warrant, include or separately draft an emergency petition with showing of imminent harm
- **Mark uncertain citations** — use `[VERIFY]` for any statute or rule number not confirmed against current code
