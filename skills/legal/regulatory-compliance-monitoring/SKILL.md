# Regulatory Compliance Monitoring

Tracks regulatory changes and assesses their impact on existing business compliance obligations.

## Prerequisites

1. **Regulatory scope** — which regulators, jurisdictions, and subject areas to monitor
2. **Current compliance baseline** — existing policies, procedures, and obligations documented in vault
3. **Stakeholder map** — who needs to know about changes in which areas

## Workflow

### 1. Monitor Sources

- Federal/state regulatory agency websites and registers
- Industry-specific regulatory feeds
- Enforcement action announcements
- Proposed rulemaking and comment periods

### 2. Assess Impact

For each relevant change:
- Compare against current compliance baseline
- Identify gaps between new requirements and existing practices
- Estimate implementation effort and timeline
- Determine if existing policies need updating

### 3. Prioritize and Alert

| Priority | Action |
|---|---|
| Immediate | New enforcement actions or emergency rules — alert compliance team same day |
| Near-term | Final rules with implementation deadlines — schedule compliance review |
| Watch | Proposed rules or guidance — track through comment period |

### 4. Document and Track

- Store regulatory updates in vault with analysis
- Maintain compliance gap tracker
- Log remediation actions and completion status

## Pitfalls

- **Don't wait for final rules** — proposed rules signal direction; start planning early
- **Check effective dates** — some rules have phased implementation or different dates by entity size
- **Cross-jurisdictional conflicts** — federal and state rules may conflict; flag for legal review
- **Enforcement trends matter** — even without rule changes, shifting enforcement priorities change risk
