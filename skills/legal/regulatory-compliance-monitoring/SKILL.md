# Regulatory Compliance Monitoring

Tracks regulatory changes and assesses their impact on existing compliance obligations.

## Prerequisites

1. **Regulatory scope** — regulators, jurisdictions, subject areas to monitor
2. **Compliance baseline** — existing policies and obligations in vault
3. **Stakeholder map** — who needs to know about changes in which areas

## Workflow

### 1. Monitor
Track: agency registers, industry feeds, enforcement actions, proposed rulemaking.

### 2. Assess
Compare each change against baseline. Identify gaps. Estimate implementation effort and timeline.

### 3. Alert
- **Immediate** — new enforcement or emergency rules, alert same day
- **Near-term** — final rules with deadlines, schedule review
- **Watch** — proposed rules, track through comment period

### 4. Document
Store updates in vault with analysis. Maintain gap tracker. Log remediation status.

## Pitfalls
- Don't wait for final rules — proposed rules signal direction
- Check effective dates — phased implementation varies by entity size
- Federal and state rules may conflict — flag for review
- Shifting enforcement priorities change risk even without new rules
