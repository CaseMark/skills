## Notes on use

- A 2026 EIS that cites 40 CFR Parts 1500–1508 as binding regulatory authority is materially incorrect. The lead agency's procedures control.
- Hard-look review survives all of the above changes — the substantive review standard applied by federal courts to NEPA documents has not changed. Page limits and deadlines have changed; the substantive analysis must still meet hard-look.
- Programmatic EISs (§ 4336b) and tiering remain available; lead-agency procedures specify mechanics.
- Project applicants may now prepare EAs and EISs themselves under § 4336a; the lead agency must still independently evaluate and approve.
