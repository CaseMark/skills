# Underwriting Agreement

Produces a firm-commitment underwriting agreement for a SEC-registered public offering.

## Prerequisites

1. **Transaction snapshot** — issuer, offering type, ticker/exchange, offering size, price range, selling stockholders.
2. **Registration materials** — effective registration statement, base prospectus, preliminary/final prospectus, permitted free writing prospectuses.
3. **Underwriting terms** — discount/commission, expense allocation, syndicate details, lock-up duration, greenshoe parameters.
4. **Closing logistics** — closing date/time, DTC settlement details, wire instructions, listing approval status.
5. **Deliverables list** — comfort letter scope, legal opinions, officer certificates, bring-down diligence, listing letter.

**Section Checklists**

Purchase and Sale
- Firm-commitment structure; obligations are several, not joint.
- Firm shares, price to public, underwriting discount, net price.
- Selling stockholder sales (if any) with allocation.

Over-Allotment Option
- Option size and term.
- Exercise mechanics and notice period.
- Allocation and settlement.

Delivery and Payment
- DTC book-entry delivery.
- Fedwire payment instructions.
- Closing sequence and location.

Representations and Warranties
- Company status, authorization, capitalization, valid issuance.
- Registration statement and prospectus accuracy; no material misstatements or omissions.
- Financial statements GAAP conformity; no material adverse change.
- Compliance with laws; no material litigation.
- Underwriters: compliance with securities laws; distribution only with permitted materials.

Covenants
- Maintain registration statement effectiveness.
- File required reports and supplements.
- No additional issuances or inconsistent disclosures.
- Use of proceeds per prospectus.
- Stabilization and distribution compliance per Regulation M.

Conditions to Closing
- Registration statement effective; no stop order.
- Reps and warranties true at closing.
- Comfort letter from auditors.
- Legal opinions: issuer counsel and underwriters' counsel.
- Listing approval and good standing certificates.
- Officer certificates and bring-down diligence.

Indemnification
- Company indemnifies underwriters for issuer-supplied disclosure.
- Underwriters indemnify company for underwriter-supplied disclosure.
- Scope of covered parties and procedures for defense.

Contribution
- Relative fault and relative benefit allocation.
- Public policy fallback if indemnity unavailable.

Termination / Market-Out
- Material adverse change in issuer or markets.
- Trading suspensions or exchange closures.
- Force majeure and legal impediments.

Lock-Up
- Covered persons; duration; permitted transfers.
- Release mechanics and notice.

Miscellaneous
- Governing law and venue.
- Assignment restrictions.
- Amendments and waivers.
- Counterparts and electronic signatures.
- Entire agreement and severability.

**Template Clauses (Insert as Needed)**

Market-Out Trigger
```
The Underwriters may terminate this Agreement by notice if, since the Execution Time,
there has occurred any event or condition that, in the judgment of the Representative,
materially adversely affects the financial markets or the business, operations, or
prospects of the Company, or trading is suspended on [Exchange] or [Major Markets].
```

Over-Allotment Option
```
The Company grants the Underwriters an option to purchase up to [15%] additional Shares
at the Public Offering Price less the underwriting discount, exercisable within [30]
days after the date hereof, solely to cover over-allotments.
```

## Guidelines

- Keep all economic terms identical to the prospectus and underwriting section disclosure.
- Ensure underwriting-related information furnished by underwriters is explicitly identified.
- Confirm DTC eligibility, listing approval, and closing mechanics before finalizing.
- Include Regulation M compliance and stabilization limitations.
- Address FINRA underwriting compensation and conflict rules where applicable (e.g., FINRA Rule 5110) [VERIFY].
- If the deal is contingent or best-efforts, do not use firm-commitment structure; assess Rule 10b-9 applicability [VERIFY].
- Use New York governing law unless the parties require a different forum.
- Replace all brackets and validate cross-references before release.
