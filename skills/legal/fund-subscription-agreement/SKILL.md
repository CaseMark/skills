# Fund Subscription Agreement

Produces a fund subscription agreement consistent with offering documents and U.S. private offering compliance.

## Prerequisites

1. Fund formation docs: PPM, LPA/LLC agreement, GP/manager entity details, side letter policy
2. Offering terms: classes/series, minimums, pricing, closings, acceptance authority, capital call mechanics
3. Investor data: legal name, entity type, address, tax status, signatory authority
4. Compliance inputs: KYC/AML checklist, W-9/W-8, accreditation evidence, OFAC screening status
5. Governing law and dispute forum preferences
6. Signature method: wet ink or e-sign, entity signature block requirements

## Output Structure / Process

### Intake Map

| Field | Required | Source | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fund legal name, entity type, jurisdiction | Yes | Formation docs | Match exact capitalization |
| Manager/GP name and address | Yes | Formation docs | Include principal place of business |
| Offering class/series | Yes | PPM | Include class rights and fees |
| Minimum subscription | Yes | PPM | Confirm any waiver authority |
| Subscription amount | Yes | Investor input | USD amount and units/shares |
| Pricing mechanics | Yes | PPM | NAV date, unit price, or flat price |
| Acceptance authority | Yes | LPA/LLC | GP/manager or committee |
| Capital calls | If applicable | LPA/LLC | Drawdown schedule or notice period |
| Transfer restrictions | Yes | LPA/LLC | Consent requirements, lockups |
| Governing law, forum | Yes | Client preference | Align to fund docs |

### Document Outline

1. Parties and Recitals
2. Definitions (if needed; align to fund docs)
3. Subscription Terms
4. Acceptance and Admission
5. Investor Representations and Warranties
6. Risk Acknowledgments
7. KYC/AML and Tax Compliance
8. Conditions Precedent
9. Payment Mechanics and Closing
10. Transfer Restrictions
11. Miscellaneous (notices, amendments, assignment, integration, severability, waiver)
12. Signature Blocks and Exhibits

### Subscription Terms Block

Include exact amounts and mechanics:
- Subscription amount in USD
- Number and class of units/shares
- Price per unit/share and valuation date
- Any commitment amount and drawdown structure
- Minimums, maximums, or oversubscription handling
- Fund right to reject or reduce subscriptions
- Binding effect only upon written acceptance

### Investor Representations Checklist

- Accredited investor status under Regulation D [VERIFY]
- Investment intent, no distribution plan
- Securities not registered; transfer restrictions acknowledged
- Illiquidity and risk of total loss accepted
- Sophistication and reliance on own advisors
- Compliance with laws applicable to investor
- Authority and capacity to sign and perform
- Not subject to disqualifying events for private offerings [VERIFY]
- Source of funds lawful; not from prohibited activities

### Risk Acknowledgments Block

Cover, at minimum:
- Market and strategy risk
- Lack of liquidity, lockup, and transfer limits
- Dependence on manager/GP and key persons
- Conflicts of interest and related party transactions
- Regulatory, tax, and valuation uncertainty
- Potential loss of entire investment

### Conditions Precedent

- Completed and accurate subscription documents
- KYC/AML and OFAC clearance
- Receipt of W-9/W-8 and tax certifications
- Verification of accreditation status (if required by offering)
- Fund acceptance by GP/manager in writing

### Payment Mechanics

- Method of payment: wire, check, or in-kind (if permitted)
- Wire instructions and payment deadline
- Treatment of failed payments or insufficient funds
- Admission timing: on acceptance or at closing
- Confirmation of ownership interest delivery

### Exhibits

- Investor Questionnaire
- Accredited Investor Verification (if used)
- Beneficial Ownership Certification (if required) [VERIFY]
- Tax Forms (W-9/W-8)
- Entity Authority Certificate or incumbency

## Guidelines

- Mirror terminology and definitions in the PPM and LPA/LLC agreement.
- Do not contradict transfer restrictions or admission criteria in fund documents.
- Include a clear acceptance clause; no admission without written acceptance.
- Confirm whether e-sign is permitted; align to ESIGN/UETA practices.
- If broker-dealers are involved, ensure compliance representations are added [VERIFY].
- Flag any jurisdiction-specific requirements for blue sky filings or exemptions.
- Use [VERIFY] for any statutory or rule citation not confirmed by source documents.
