# Title Commitment and Policy

Drafts a complete ALTA-standard Title Commitment that identifies the state of title, specifies requirements for policy issuance, and enumerates exceptions to coverage.

## Prerequisites

1. **Property information** — legal description, APN, county, state
2. **Transaction details** — purchase price or loan amount, policy type (Owner's/Loan/Leasehold), closing date
3. **Current vesting** — present owner(s) of record, recording info for current deed
4. **Title search results** — encumbrances, liens, easements, CC&Rs, judgments discovered in examination
5. **Party information** — proposed insured(s) with full legal names and entity designations
6. **Existing encumbrances** — mortgages, deeds of trust, tax status, assessments to be addressed at closing

## Output Structure

### Document Header

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Title | "COMMITMENT FOR TITLE INSURANCE" — prominent formatting |
| Issuing company | Full legal name, licensed in relevant jurisdiction |
| Commitment number | Format: `[YEAR]-[OFFICE CODE]-[SEQ]` |
| Effective date | Date through which title search was conducted |

### Schedule A — Transaction Details

Draft in this order:

1. **Policy type** — ALTA Owner's Policy (2006), ALTA Loan Policy, or ALTA Leasehold Policy
2. **Policy amount** — exact dollar figure (purchase price for owner's; loan amount for lender's)
3. **Proposed insured** — full legal names with entity designations; for multiple parties, specify interests/ownership percentages
4. **Current vesting** — owner(s) of record as shown on most recent deed, with recording reference (instrument number, book/page)
5. **Legal description** — verbatim from most recent deed or survey; use jurisdiction-appropriate format (metes and bounds, lot/block, government survey); include plat/subdivision recording references; delineate multiple parcels separately

### Schedule B, Part I — Requirements

Enumerate all conditions for policy issuance in closing-timeline order:

| # | Requirement Category | Details to Include |
|---|---------------------|--------------------|
| 1 | Premium and fees | Payment of title insurance premium |
| 2 | Purchase price / loan funding | Confirmation via settlement statement and funds transfer |
| 3 | Conveyance instrument | Warranty/grant deed executed, acknowledged, and recorded |
| 4 | Existing lien releases | Payoff statements + recorded satisfactions for all mortgages, DOTs, judgment liens, tax liens not surviving closing |
| 5 | Owner's affidavit | Parties in possession, mechanic's liens, unrecorded interests, recent improvements |
| 6 | Entity authority docs | Good standing certificates, authorizing resolutions, incumbency certificates (if entity parties) |
| 7 | Survey | Current ALTA/NSPS survey if required to remove standard survey exception |
| 8 | Gap coverage | Any gap indemnity or bring-down search requirements |

### Schedule B, Part II — Exceptions to Coverage

Introductory statement: policy will not insure against loss from the following unless removed or modified prior to closing.

**Standard Exceptions** (remove if affirmatively covered by endorsement):

- [ ] Rights/claims of parties in possession not disclosed by public records
- [ ] Easements or claims of easements not shown by public records
- [ ] Encroachments, boundary variations, or adverse circumstances discoverable by survey/inspection
- [ ] Mechanic's/materialmen's liens not yet of record
- [ ] Taxes or assessments not yet shown as liens

**Specific Exceptions** — list each with full detail:

| Type | Required Detail |
|------|----------------|
| Real property taxes | Current year status; prior year payment confirmation |
| CC&Rs | Complete recording reference; subdivision/condo/HOA designation |
| Easements of record | Holder, purpose (utility/access/drainage/conservation), location, recording ref; note appurtenant vs. servient |
| Surviving mortgages/DOTs | Lender, original principal, recording ref, estimated current balance |
| Judgments/liens | Creditor, amount, recording ref, whether attaches to property or owner's interest only |
| Lis pendens/litigation | Case number, court, nature of action |
| Special assessments/CDD bonds | Assessing authority, purpose, total amount, payment status |
| Mineral/subsurface rights | Scope of severance, recording reference for reservation |

## Guidelines

- Follow current ALTA form standards; specify form version in Schedule A
- Spell out months in all dates; use `$X,XXX,XXX.00` format for dollar amounts
- Every recorded document reference must include sufficient locator info for the jurisdiction (instrument number OR book/page OR official record number)
- Maintain exact consistency in party names, property descriptions, and recording references throughout
- Distinguish clearly between requirements that must be satisfied *before* closing vs. *at* closing vs. *after* closing
- If assuming existing financing, except the surviving lien in Schedule B, Part II — do not list it as a requirement to release
- Flag any gap between effective date and anticipated closing date; note whether gap coverage or bring-down search is needed
- Do not draft endorsements inline — note which standard exceptions can be removed by endorsement and list the applicable ALTA endorsement form number
- Mark any citation or statutory reference that cannot be verified as `[VERIFY]`
