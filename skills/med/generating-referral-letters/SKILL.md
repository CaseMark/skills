# Generating Referral Letters

Creates structured specialist referral communications.

## Workflow

1. **Identify** referral type — consultation, co-management, transfer of care
2. **Select** relevant history — condition-specific, not full chart dump
3. **State** specific question — what is the referring provider asking
4. **Attach** relevant data references — recent labs, imaging, prior treatment
5. **Indicate** urgency and preferred timeframe

## Output Template

```markdown
## Referral Letter

**Date**: [Date]
**To**: [Specialist / Department]
**From**: [Referring Provider]
**Re**: [Patient Name, DOB]
**Urgency**: [Routine / Urgent / Emergent]

Dear [Provider],

I am referring [patient demographics] for evaluation of [specific concern].

### Relevant History
[Concise pertinent history — 2-4 paragraphs max]

### Current Medications
[Relevant medications only]

### Consultation Question
[Specific question(s) for the specialist]

### Enclosed
- [List of attached records, labs, imaging]

Thank you for your evaluation. Please contact me with questions.

Sincerely,
[Referring Provider]
```

## Key Rules

- Lead with the consultation question — specialists read this first
- Include only relevant history, not comprehensive chart summary
- Always specify urgency and preferred timeframe
- List specific enclosed documents
- Avoid diagnostic conclusions outside your expertise — describe findings instead
