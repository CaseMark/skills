# Reviewing IRB Submissions

Assesses research protocols for IRB readiness.

## Workflow

1. **Check** protocol completeness — required sections present
2. **Evaluate** risk classification — exempt, expedited, or full board review
3. **Assess** informed consent — all required elements per 45 CFR 46.116
4. **Review** subject protections — vulnerable populations, data safety, adverse event reporting
5. **Identify** gaps and required revisions

## Key Rules

- Vulnerable populations (children, prisoners, pregnant women, cognitively impaired) trigger additional protections
- HIPAA authorization is separate from informed consent — check both
- Data safety monitoring plan required for interventional studies
- Conflict of interest disclosure must be assessed
- International studies may require local IRB approval in addition
