# E/M Level Determination Guide

## Office/Outpatient E/M (99202-99215) — 2021+ Guidelines

E/M level is based on **Medical Decision Making (MDM)** OR **Total Time** (provider choice).

### MDM-Based Selection

| Level | # Problems | Data Reviewed | Risk |
|-------|-----------|--------------|------|
| **99211** | — | — | — (nurse visit, no MDM required) |
| **99202/12** | 1 self-limited problem | Minimal or none | Minimal risk |
| **99203/13** | 2+ self-limited OR 1 chronic stable | Limited (order/review tests, obtain history from external) | Low risk (OTC drugs, minor surgery w/o risk factors) |
| **99204/14** | 1+ chronic with exacerbation OR 2+ chronic stable OR 1 undiagnosed new problem | Moderate (order/review tests with independent interpretation, discussion with external physician) | Moderate risk (Rx drug management, minor surgery w/ risk factors, IV fluids) |
| **99205/15** | 1+ chronic with severe exacerbation OR 1 acute/chronic threatening life | Extensive (independent interpretation of tests + discussion with external physician + independent historian) | High risk (hospitalization, drugs requiring intensive monitoring, decision for major surgery/DNR) |

### Time-Based Selection

| Level | New Patient | Established Patient |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| 99202/99212 | 15-29 min | 10-19 min |
| 99203/99213 | 30-44 min | 20-29 min |
| 99204/99214 | 45-59 min | 30-39 min |
| 99205/99215 | 60-74 min | 40-54 min |
| + prolonged | Each add'l 15 min: +99417 | Each add'l 15 min: +99417 |

**Time includes**: reviewing records, ordering tests, documenting, care coordination, counseling — on the date of encounter.

### MDM Element Details

**Number and Complexity of Problems**:
- Self-limited = resolves on its own (URI, insect bite)
- Chronic stable = ongoing but controlled (HTN on stable meds)
- Chronic exacerbation = ongoing and worsening
- Undiagnosed new problem = workup needed to determine

**Data Reviewed** (need 2 of 3 categories for "moderate", unique combination for "extensive"):
1. Tests ordered/reviewed
2. Independent interpretation of tests (not just reading the report)
3. Discussion with external physician / obtaining external records / independent historian

**Risk of Complications** — highest-risk element drives the level.

## Inpatient/Observation E/M

| Code | Use |
|------|-----|
| 99221-99223 | Initial hospital care (new or established patient) |
| 99231-99233 | Subsequent hospital care |
| 99234-99236 | Observation or inpatient same-day admit/discharge |
| 99238-99239 | Hospital discharge day management |

## Common Documentation Gaps

| Gap | Impact | Fix |
|-----|--------|-----|
| "Patient doing well" without specifics | Cannot determine problem status | Document: which problems, stable/improved/worsened |
| "Reviewed labs" without interpretation | Doesn't count as independent interpretation | Document: what was reviewed, clinical significance |
| No risk discussion | Cannot assess MDM risk level | Document: treatment decisions, drug monitoring plan |
| Time not documented | Cannot use time-based billing | Document total time and activities performed |
